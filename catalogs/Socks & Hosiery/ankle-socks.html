Making Process

Ankle socks demand precise engineering to deliver comfort and durability at scale. Here's how we manufacture:

  • Yarn Selection: We use only combed cotton for superior softness and consistency, blended with polyester for durability and elastane for stretch retention through multiple washes.
  • Knitting: Circular knitting machines calibrated for tight, even weaves; reinforced heel and toe zones receive extra passes to prevent premature wear.
  • Elastic Rib Cuffs: Engineered rib cuffs that stay in place without cutting off circulation, ensuring comfort for all-day wear.
  • Boarding & Sizing: Heat-boarding locks shape and prevents unraveling; each size is verified against your chart before proceeding.
  • Colorfast Dyeing: Proprietary dyeing processes ensure deep, even colors that withstand multiple washes; AQL checks on seams and stretch prevent defects.
  • Final Inspection: 100% pair-matching, length verification, and visual inspection before packing.
