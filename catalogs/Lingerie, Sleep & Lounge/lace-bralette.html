Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify band and cup sizing, strap placement, lace placement/pattern, back closure (hook or pullover), and stretch recovery. Fit samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 85% nylon / 15% spandex with delicate lace overlay (180 GSM) for stretch and comfort.

Lace: Quality lace applied to nylon-spandex base; open or sheer designs per pattern spec.

Construction: Wireless design for natural support; adjustable straps per spec; hook back closure or pullover style.

Comfort Features: Soft waistband; reinforced seams; lined or unlined cups per design.

Finishing: Gentle wash maintaining lace integrity; stretch recovery testing; QC for fit accuracy and lace quality.

Sourcing

Compliant nylon-spandex mills; quality lace; hooks and elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per size/color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
