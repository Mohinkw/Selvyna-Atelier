Premium Opaque Tights Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack Creation

Opaque tights demand precision documentation to achieve consistent coverage and finish. We develop detailed tech packs that specify knit density parameters, waistband tension, denier targets, opacity standards (e.g., 100% coverage at all angles), size charts with fit allowances, wash/care protocols, and packaging specifications. Our tech packs reference industry standards so your finished goods meet or exceed customer expectations for opacity and comfort.

Sampling

Fit and opacity samples arrive within 8�10 days. We evaluate coverage consistency, waistband comfort (no rolling or digging), color accuracy, ladder resistance, and overall handfeel. Our samples are wash-tested to ensure opacity and color hold through multiple launderings. Adjustments to waistband height, opacity level, or finish (matte vs. slight sheen) are iterated quickly.

Making Process

Opaque tights combine technical knitting with luxury feel. Our manufacturing process includes premium nylon and spandex blends for opacity and durability; fine-denier knitting ensures uniform coverage with no see-through areas; engineered waistbands prevent rolling while maintaining all-day comfort; ladder-resistance testing confirms durability; deep, saturated dyeing creates rich shades; and optional matte or slight-sheen finishes match brand aesthetics.

Sourcing

Opaque tights demand premium materials. We source high-quality nylon and spandex from trusted mills, waistband elastics tuned for comfort and durability, reinforced gusset materials, luxury dyes for depth and colorfastness, labels, and packaging. Our supply chain is fully transparent�every component is tracked and validated to support your brand promise of quality and luxury feel.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): Flexible Order Options
