Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist, inseam, cargo pocket placement/size, pocket styles, waistband type, and shrink allowances. Fit and pocket tests in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton twill (200 GSM) for durability and structure.

Cargo Pockets: Multiple cargo pockets with flap closures; reinforced pocket openings; thigh and side pockets per design.

Fit: High-waist or mid-waist per spec; relaxed through hip; short length.

Construction: Button/zip fly waistband; multiple pockets (front, back, cargo); topstitched throughout.

Finishing: Light wash or color setting; controlled shrink; QC for pocket placement accuracy and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton twill mills; quality buttons; pocket hardware; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.
