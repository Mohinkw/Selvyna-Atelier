Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist, length, pleat depth/placement, waistband type, built-in short/lining, stretch recovery targets, and shrink allowances. Fit and stretch tests in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 88% polyester / 12% spandex (220 GSM) for performance and recovery.

Pleats: Sharp front pleats with reinforced stitching; press set for durability; consistent across all sizes.

Fit: High-rise waist per athletic proportions; short length or midi per spec; built-in shorts or separate lining.

Construction: Elastic back waist; topstitched pleats; reinforced for active movement.

Finishing: Stretch recovery wash; pleat setting; QC for pleat sharpness, recovery, and measurements.
