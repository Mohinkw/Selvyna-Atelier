Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist, inseam, drawstring spec, pocket placement, waistband type, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% polyester French terry (260 GSM) for soft comfort and structure.

Fit: Elasticated drawstring waist; relaxed through hip and thigh; short length.

Construction: Drawstring closure with waistband; front pockets; relaxed side seams.

Finishing: Controlled wash maintaining softness; gentle press; QC for measurements and terry quality.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton-polyester French terry mills; quality drawstring; labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.
