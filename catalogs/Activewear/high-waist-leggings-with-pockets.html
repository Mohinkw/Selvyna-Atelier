Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs cover rise height, inseam, waistband tension maps, pocket placement templates, fabric GSM, and stitch specs. Fit and handfeel samples in 10-12 days with variants for compression level, waistband tension, and pocket depth to validate bounce-free storage.

Making Process

Yarn & Fabric Selection: Premium nylon/spandex blends (85/15, 200-260 GSM) tested for stretch recovery, opacity, and colorfastness. Dye lots are lab-dipped and shade-banded before knitting.

Seamless Knitting & Pocket Integration: Circular seamless knitting builds tubes with zero side seams. Pockets are engineered into the knit or applied with reinforced bartacks to hold phones during motion. Waistbands use graduated compression to prevent rolling.

Waistband Engineering: Non-roll waistbands with optional drawstrings or bonded elastic; tension tuned per size curve to stay secure without discomfort.

Boarding & Heat Setting: Steam boarding locks silhouette and length, preventing shrink and keeping pocket alignment consistent across lots.

Final Inspection: AQL Level 2 with stretch-recovery tests, pocket seam strength pulls, waistband stability checks, needle damage scans, and shade review under D65 light.

Sourcing

Traceable nylon and spandex yarns from certified mills; moisture-wicking finishes and threads from compliant suppliers; labels and trims sourced with social and environmental audit's.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color. Volume pricing unlocks at 1,000+ pcs per color for wholesale and FBA programs.
