Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist/hip/thigh/leg opening measurements, inseam, waistband type (drawstring or elastic), pocket placement, print/color, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% rayon gauze (100 GSM) for maximum breathability and light weight.

Relaxed Fit: Generous through hip and thigh; wide leg opening; vacation proportions.

Waistband: Drawstring or elasticated per spec; comfortable fit for travel wear.

Construction: Side pockets or no pockets per design; minimal seaming; traditional or flat seams.

Finishing: Gentle wash; minimal pressing; QC for measurements and print consistency (if applicable).

Sourcing

Compliant rayon gauze mills; elastic or drawstring cord; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per print/color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.20 - $5.60 including rayon gauze, waistband/elastic, seam work, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 19-23 days after approvals. Print applications add 2-3 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Fold protects delicate fabric; optional tissue.
