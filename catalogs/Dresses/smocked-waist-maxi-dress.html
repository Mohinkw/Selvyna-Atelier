Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define smocking rows, stitch density, elastic gauge, panel lengths, sweep, lining options, and shrink allowances. Strike-offs and fit samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: Rayon challis, viscose, or poly crepe (120-140 GSM) for drape. Pre-wash to control shrink and torque.

Cutting: Panel matching for prints; grain-true cutting to keep skirt fall smooth.

Smocking: Calibrated elastic tension for comfort without digging; consistent row spacing.

Construction: Clean finishing on seams; optional French seams for light colors; lined bodice where needed.

Quality Checks: Smock elasticity tests, print alignment, sweep measurements, and AQL Level 2 inspection.

Sourcing

Dyed and printed challis from certified mills; colorfastness and crocking tested; trims and elastics from compliant suppliers.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color. Mixed print packs possible with shared fabric base.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.36 - $9.44 including fabric, smocking, lining, QC, and packing. Prints or specialty finishes may add $0.40 - $0.70.

Production Time

Bulk: 26-31 days after approvals. Prints add 5-7 days for strike-offs and lab dips.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Individually bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert cards. Fold strategy to minimize crush lines on smocking.
