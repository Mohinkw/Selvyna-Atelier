Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, inseam length, leg width, neckline, sleeve length, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 70% linen / 30% rayon blend (140 GSM) for breathability and elegant drape.

Fit: Relaxed through body; straight or slightly tapered legs; comfortable inseam per design.

Neckline: Crew neck, V-neck, or button-up front per spec; long or short sleeves.

Details: Side seam pockets; optional waist drawstring; simple topstitched or flatlock seams.

Finishing: Controlled shrink preserving linen texture; gentle press; QC for measurements and fabric hand.

Sourcing

Compliant linen-rayon blend mills; quality buttons or elastic; labels within RSL.
