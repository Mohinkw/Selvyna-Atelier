Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define neckline width, elastic gauge, body length, sleeve shape, rib trims, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to validate hold and drape.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 80% acrylic / 20% poly at 9-10 gauge for soft drape and light warmth.

Neck Stability: Elasticized or ribbed neck tuned to stay in place without digging.

Construction: Linked shoulders; rib cuffs/hem; optional brush wash for extra softness.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for elastic comfort, measurements, and shade.

Sourcing

Compliant yarn mills; elastics within RSL; trims/labels traceable.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.50 - $9.38 including yarn, elastic/neck rib, knitting, linking, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging
