Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify length, upper arm circumference, forearm circumference, compression level (mmHg rating if applicable), and whether graduated or uniform compression. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 85% nylon / 15% spandex (200 GSM) engineered for consistent compression and recovery support.

Compression Engineering: Graduated or uniform compression depending on spec; engineered yarn tension provides support.

Construction: Seamless tube knit or minimal seam construction to prevent chafing during wear.

Comfort Features: Moisture-wicking properties; breathable mesh ventilation options; soft inner lining possible.

Finishing: Quality cuffs; flatlock seaming where applicable; QC for compression consistency.

Compliant high-compression nylon-spandex mills; specialized elastic materials; moisture-wicking additives; labels within RSL.
