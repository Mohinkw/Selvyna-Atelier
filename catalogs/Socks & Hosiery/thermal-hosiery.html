Tech Pack Creation

Thermal hosiery require specialized tech packs detailing yarn specs, brushing method protocols, knit density parameters for warmth retention, size charts with precise fit allowances, care instructions emphasizing thermal properties, packaging specifications, and carton requirements. Our documentation ensures that every pair delivers consistent warmth and comfort through production.

Sampling

Thermal handfeel and fit'samples arrive within 10?12 days with brushing options to validate warmth level, stretch, opacity, and overall comfort. We evaluate handfeel consistency, brushing quality, color accuracy, and durability through wear testing. Adjustments to brushing intensity or yarn blend are iterated quickly with revised samples.

Making Process

Thermal hosiery combine warmth with comfort through cotton and polyester yarn blending for thermal efficiency; spandex for stretch and recovery; strategic knit density tuning for opacity and insulation; interior brushing and fleece-lining creating air pockets for heat retention; boarding stabilizing fit and improving handfeel; AQL checks on seams, pilling resistance, and thermal properties; and rigorous quality assurance.

Sourcing

Thermal hosiery demand specialized materials. We source cotton/poly/spandex fleece yarns engineered for warmth without bulk, brushing and finishing chemicals for loft and softness, fleece-lining materials, dyes optimized for thermal fabrics, labels highlighting thermal properties, and packaging designed for seasonal packaging. Our supply chain is transparent and compliant, supporting your brand's winter positioning and customer satisfaction.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): Flexible Order Options
