Tech Pack & Sampling

Construction: Five-pocket configuration; reinforced rivet placement; sturdy YKK button; topstitched seams with double-stitched inseams.

Finishing: Selected wash process; leg shaping; shrink control; QC for measurements and fade evenness.

Compliant denim mills; rust-free rivets; YKK zippers; nickel-safe buttons; labels within RSL.
