Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify rib gauge, strap width, neck depth, body length, shrink allowances, and label placement. Fit'samples in 8-10 days to validate recovery and coverage.

Making Process

Fabric: 190-210 GSM cotton/spandex rib for recovery and comfort; shade-banded lots.

Cut & Sew: Grain-true cutting to keep ribs straight; elasticized or self-straps with bartacks; twin-needle or coverstitch hems.

Stability: Stay-stitch at neck; optional clear elastic at neckline for hold.

Finishing: Steam to spec; QC for recovery, neck symmetry, and shade.

Sourcing

Combed cotton ribs from compliant mills; elastics and trims nickel-safe; dyes meeting RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 600 pcs per color. Shared fabric base across multiple neck depths possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.25 - $3.00 including fabric, rib binding, bartacks, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-21 days after approvals. Rush feasible on repeat colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Neck prints or woven labels available.
