Premium Puff Sleeve Blouse Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define sleeve volume, elastic channel width, neck shape, bust darts, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 10-12 days to validate sleeve balance and coverage.

Making Process

Fabric: 110-130 GSM cotton poplin or light twill for crisp yet soft handfeel.

Cutting: Grain-true cutting; optional print matching on yokes or plackets.

Construction: Stay-stitched necklines; clean facings or binding; elastic inserted with controlled tension; seams overlocked and pressed.

Finishing: Sleeve press to set volume; QC for sleeve symmetry, neck flatness, and shade.

Sourcing

Poplins from compliant mills; elastics and interfacing tested for recovery; buttons nickel-safe; dyes within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color. Sleeve length variations can share body.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.50 - $6.00 including fabric, elastic channels, facings, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals. Embroidery or prints add 3-5 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding keeps sleeves uncrushed; tissue optional.

Selvyna Atelier Advantage

  • Balanced Volume: Sleeve ratios tuned to avoid collapse or over-puff.
  • Neck Stability: Stay-stitched necklines resist ripple.
  • Elastic Comfort: Calibrated tension for comfort and longevity.
  • QC Transparency: Sleeve symmetry and shade reports per lot.

Why Choose Bangladesh for Puff Blouses?

  • Pattern Accuracy: Skilled teams for structured sleeves.
  • Cost Efficiency: Competitive FOB on woven blouses.
  • Agile Sampling: Fast revisions on sleeve and neck shapes.
  • Compliance Ready: Socially audited production for exports.
