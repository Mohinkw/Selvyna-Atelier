Our Certifications & Standards

Committed to excellence, ethics, and quality. Our certifications demonstrate our dedication to international standards and responsible manufacturing practices.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

What is ISO 9001?

ISO 9001 is an internationally recognized quality management system (QMS) standard that ensures businesses consistently provide products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements. It focuses on continuous improvement, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.

Why is this Certification Important?

ISO 9001 certification demonstrates our commitment to:

  • Maintaining consistent quality in every garment we produce
  • Implementing rigorous quality control processes
  • Ensuring customer satisfaction through systematic improvement
  • Meeting international standards and regulatory requirements
  • Building trust with global brands and retailers

Our Commitment

Selvyna Atelier proudly holds ISO 9001:2015 certification, demonstrating our dedication to delivering premium quality women's apparel with consistent excellence. Our certified QMS ensures every production stagefrom design to deliverymeets the highest international standards.

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

What is OEKO-TEX Standard 100?

OEKO-TEX Standard 100 is one of the world's leading textile certifications for harmful substances. It tests fabrics at all processing stages to ensure they are free from harmful chemicals and safe for human health, including sensitive skin.

Why is this Certification Important?

OEKO-TEX certification is crucial because it:

  • Guarantees textiles are free from harmful substances and safe for skin contact
  • Protects consumers, especially those with sensitive skin or allergies
  • Ensures compliance with strict international safety regulations
  • Demonstrates commitment to sustainable and responsible production
  • Provides transparency and trust in the textile supply chain

Our Commitment

We are OEKO-TEX certified, ensuring that all fabrics and materials used in our garments meet the highest safety standards. Your customers can wear our products with confidence, knowing they are safe, eco-friendly, and free from harmful chemicals.

BSCI (Business Social Compliance Initiative)

What is BSCI?

BSCI is a leading business-driven initiative for improving working conditions in global supply chains. It focuses on social compliance, ensuring fair labor practices, worker safety, and ethical business operations in manufacturing facilities.

Why is this Certification Important?

BSCI certification is essential for:

  • Protecting workers' rights and ensuring fair wages
  • Providing safe and healthy working conditions
  • Preventing child labor and forced labor
  • Promoting ethical and responsible sourcing practices
  • Meeting corporate social responsibility standards required by global brands

Our Commitment

Selvyna Atelier is BSCI certified, reflecting our unwavering commitment to ethical manufacturing. We prioritize the we'll-being of our workforce, ensuring fair treatment, safe working environments, and dignified employment for all team members.

GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)

What is GOTS?

GOTS is the worldwide leading textile processing standard for organic fibers. It defines high-level environmental criteria along the entire organic textiles supply chain and requires compliance with social criteria as we'll.

Why is this Certification Important?

GOTS certification ensures:

  • Organic fibers are processed and manufactured sustainably
  • Harmful chemicals and toxic dyes are eliminated from production
  • Environmental impact is minimized through eco-friendly practices
  • Social responsibility standards are maintained throughout the supply chain
  • End consumers receive genuinely organic and sustainable products

Our Commitment

We hold GOTS certification for our organic product lines, demonstrating our dedication to sustainable fashion. From organic cotton sourcing to eco-friendly dyeing and finishing, we ensure minimal environmental impact while delivering premium quality organic apparel.

Sedex Membership

What is Sedex?

Sedex (Supplier Ethical Data Exchange) is one of the world's leading ethical trade membership organizations. It provides a platform for companies to manage and improve working conditions in global supply chains, focusing on labor standards, health & safety, environment, and business ethics.

Why is this Certification Important?

Sedex membership demonstrates:

  • Transparency in supply chain practices and ethical operations
  • Commitment to continuous improvement in social responsibility
  • Compliance with international labor and environmental standards
  • Regular audit's and monitoring of working conditions
  • Building trust with global brands that require ethical sourcing

Our Commitment

As a Sedex member, Selvyna Atelier maintains full transparency in our operations. We undergo regular ethical audit's and share detailed information about our labor practices, environmental impact, and business ethics, ensuring accountability at every level.

BGMEA Membership

What is BGMEA?

The Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association (BGMEA) is the apex trade body of the readymade garment industry in Bangladesh. It represents thousands of manufacturers and ensures compliance with international standards, trade regulations, and best practices.

Why is this Certification Important?

BGMEA membership is vital because it:

  • Ensures compliance with Bangladesh labor laws and international standards
  • Provides access to global trade networks and export facilities
  • Guarantees adherence to factory safety and building codes
  • Offers credibility and trust in the Bangladesh garment industry
  • Supports continuous improvement and industry best practices

Our Commitment

Selvyna Atelier is a proud BGMEA member, operating with full legal compliance and industry recognition. Our membership ensures we adhere to the highest standards of safety, quality, and ethical manufacturing practices established by Bangladesh's premier garment industry association.
