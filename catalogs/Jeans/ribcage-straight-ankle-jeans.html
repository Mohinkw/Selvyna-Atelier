Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define rise height, waist/hip/thigh/knee/ankle measurements, inseam, pocket depth, button/rivet spec, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 98% cotton / 2% elastane 11oz denim (330 GSM) for stretch and recovery.

Fit: Straight thigh with tapered ankle; mid-rise waist; five-pocket construction.

Construction: Reinforced rivet placement; YKK button; topstitched seams; double-stitched inseams for durability.

Finishing: Wash and fade per spec; shrink control; leg shaping; QC for measurements and consistency.
