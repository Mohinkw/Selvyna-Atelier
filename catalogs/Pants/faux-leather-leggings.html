Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify high-waist or mid-waist, length, waist/hip/thigh measurements, glossy finish spec, stretch recovery targets, and shrink allowances. Fit and recovery tests in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 90% polyester / 10% spandex faux leather (240 GSM) with glossy coating.

Form Fit: Tapered through leg; high-rise or mid-rise waist per spec; second-skin proportions.

Waistband: Elasticated or fold-over top; reinforced for comfort on tight fit.

Construction: Flatlock or hidden seams to minimize bulk; sturdy stitching for stretch fabric.

Finishing: Stretch recovery wash; gloss coating application; QC for stretch, gloss, and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant faux leather mills; high-quality spandex; glossy coatings; elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.55 - $5.95 including faux leather, spandex blend, gloss coating, waistband, seam work, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals.
