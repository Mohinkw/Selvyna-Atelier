Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs capture bias angle, neckline shape, strap width, lining options, and hem flare. Fit and wear-test samples in 10-12 days to validate bias hang and shine.

Making Process

Fabric: 80-120 GSM satin (poly or viscose) with peach or sand wash for softness and reduced static.

Cutting: True bias cutting with nesting to reduce twist; panel blocking for color consistency.

Construction: Clean finishes: narrow rolled hems, French seams or overlock + topstitch; bartack at straps.

Finishing: Light steam on forms to set drape; QC for side seam hang and neck symmetry.

Sourcing

Satin from certified mills; colorfastness and snag resistance tested; trims and rings/sliders nickel-safe.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color. Mixed necklines can share fabric base.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.04 - $8.72 including fabric, bias cutting, clean seams, QC, and packing. Specialty washes add $0.30 - $0.60.

Production Time

Bulk: 22-26 days after approvals. Rush windows possible on repeat colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert cards. Folding strategy prevents cowl creases; optional tissue interleave.
