Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify hoodie chest width, length, sleeve length, hood style, zipper/drawstring placement; joggers waist, rise, inseam, and cuff type. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% polyester fleece (300 GSM) for warmth, softness, and durability.

Coordination: Hoodie and joggers matched in color and fleece weight for cohesive tracksuit aesthetic.

Hoodie: Comfortable fit with engineered hood; quality zipper or drawstring; kangaroo pocket optional; quality ribbed cuffs.

Joggers: Comfortable waist with drawstring; relaxed leg taper; quality ankle cuffs; finished seams.

Finishing: Soft press without breaking fleece; flatlock or quality seaming; reinforced drawstring channels; QC for color matching and durability.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton-polyester fleece mills; quality zippers or drawstrings; color matching verified; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 sets per color.
