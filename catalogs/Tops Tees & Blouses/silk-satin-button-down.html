Premium Silk/Satin Button Down Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define collar shape, placket width, cuff style, back pleats, hem curve, and shrink allowances. Fit and shine samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 110-130 GSM satin (poly/spandex or viscose) with soft wash to reduce static and cling.

Cutting: True grain cutting for drape; panel blocking to maintain shade.

Construction: Clean plackets, edge-stitch where required; French seams or narrow overlock; reinforced button stands.

Finishing: Light form steam for collar roll; QC for shine uniformity, seam puckering, and measurements.

Sourcing

Satin from compliant mills; buttons dyed-to-match; interlinings with low shrink; trims nickel-safe.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color. Mixed sleeve lengths can share base body.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.88 - $6.38 including fabric, interlining, clean seams, buttons, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals. Prints add 5-7 days for strike-offs.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Folded and bagged with barcode/ASIN, size stickers, hang tags. Collar support to preserve roll; optional tissue.
