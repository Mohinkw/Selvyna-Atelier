Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify dimensions (length and width), hem type (rolled, flat, decorative), print or weave pattern, edge finish, and packaging. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% rayon challis (110 GSM) for soft hand feel and elegant drape.

Design: Custom prints, weaves, or solid colors per specification.

Hems: Rolled hem, flat hem, or decorative stitch per design (typically 0.25-0.5 inches).

Edge Finishing: Quality edge finishing to prevent fraying; clean appearance throughout.

Quality Control: Print/weave quality, color accuracy, hem evenness, and fold testing.

Sourcing

Compliant rayon challis mills; quality dyes and printing/weaving facilities; labels within RSL.
