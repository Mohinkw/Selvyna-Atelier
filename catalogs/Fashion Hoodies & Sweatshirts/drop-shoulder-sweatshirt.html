Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define shoulder drop, body/sleeve length, neckline shape, rib widths, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 9-11 days to validate drop and comfort.

Making Process

Fabric: 85% cotton / 15% poly french terry (270 GSM) for softness and durability.

Shoulder Drop: Cut to specific shoulder point drop; balanced shoulder seams prevent rolling.

Construction: Crewneck rib with tuned ratio; rib cuffs and hem; linked shoulders; seams overlocked or topstitched.

Finishing: Shrink control wash; light steam; QC for measurements and shade.

Sourcing

Compliant mills for french terry; trims and labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
