Premium Leg Warmers Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify length, calf circumference, ankle circumference, elastic type, seam style, and whether slits are desired on the sides. Fit samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% acrylic / 20% polyester knit (260 GSM) for extra warmth and durability.

Construction: Seamless tube knit or flat-seamed per design; proportioned for full leg length.

Cuff Design: Elasticated cuffs at ankle and above knee per spec.

Side Slits: Optional side slits from ankle to knee area for ease of movement (per design).

Finishing: Soft cozy finish; quality seaming; comfortable cuffs; QC for leg length consistency.

Sourcing

Compliant acrylic-polyester mills with heavier weight; quality elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pairs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $1.40 - $2.10 per pair including acrylic-polyester knit (260 GSM), seamless or flat-seam construction, elasticated cuffs, optional side slits, soft finishing, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging
