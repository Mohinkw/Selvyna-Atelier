Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define rise, relaxed waist/hip/thigh/leg opening measurements, inseam, rip placement/size, raw edge styling, button/rivet spec, and shrink allowances. Fit and distressing samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 99% cotton / 1% spandex 11.5oz denim (345 GSM) for structure with minimal stretch.

Distressing: Strategic knife cuts at knees and thighs; controlled fraying; optional whiskering on thighs.

Fit: Relaxed through hip and thigh; straight leg; boyfriend proportions.

Construction: Five-pocket configuration; reinforced rivet placement; YKK button; topstitched seams with reinforced rip areas.

Finishing: Selected wash with distress enhancement; shrink control; QC for rip consistency and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant denim mills; rust-free rivets; YKK zippers; nickel-safe buttons; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per wash.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.12 - $9.00 including denim, five-pocket construction, distressing labor, hardware, wash, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 27-31 days after approvals. Distressing and wash processing add 7-10 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Button and zipper protected; optional tissue.
