Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder seam placement, chest/bust width, vest length, armhole depth, zipper placement, and pocket placement. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Shell Fabric: 100% nylon taffeta (120 GSM) for water resistance and durability.

Fill: Polyester insulation (220 GSM) providing warmth-to-weight ratio optimized for packing.

Construction: Channel-quilted design locking fill in place; reinforced seams throughout.

Details: Front zipper closure with wind flap; side pockets; adjustable hem drawstring per design.

Finishing: Heat-sealed seams preventing fill escape; careful press; QC for loft uniformity and temperature performance.
