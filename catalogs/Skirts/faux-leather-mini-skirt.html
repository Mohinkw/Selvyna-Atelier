Making Process

Fabric: 90% polyester / 10% spandex faux leather (220 GSM) with glossy coating.

Fit: High-waist or mid-waist per spec; fitted silhouette; mini length.

Waistband: Elasticated or zip back; reinforced for fitted proportions.

Construction: Side seams or minimal seaming; hidden or topstitched details per design.

Finishing: Gloss coating application; QC for glossiness and measurements.
