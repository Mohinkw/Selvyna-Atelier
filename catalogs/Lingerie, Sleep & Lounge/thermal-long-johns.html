Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify top length, pant inseam, waistband type, cuff style, insulation targets, and shrink allowances. Fit and thermal samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% polyester thermal knit (220-240 GSM) with insulating hollow fibers.

Construction: Waffle knit or thermal grid pattern for air trapping; long sleeves; full-length pant legs.

Waistband: Elasticated waist with drawstring per spec; reinforced for durability.

Cuffs: Ribbed or tapered cuffs at wrist and ankle to seal in warmth.

Finishing: Controlled shrink maintaining thermal properties; seam inspection for heat retention; QC for measurements and insulation quality.
