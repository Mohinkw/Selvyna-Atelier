Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define strap mapping, cup shapes, elastic widths, stitch specs, pad pocket placement, and GSM. Fit samples in 10-12 days with strap tension options and cup thickness variants to validate support and comfort.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 88% Polyester / 12% Spandex (220-240 GSM) with wicking finish; mesh lining options for breathability.

Strap Engineering: Elasticized straps with bartacks at stress points; adjustable or fixed options; tension tuned per size curve to prevent digging.

Cup & Pad Construction: Removable pads with clean-finish openings; optional molded cups; underbust elastic stabilized to avoid flip or curl.

Seaming & Finishing: Flatlock or coverstitch seams for chafe-free wear; clean armhole binding for comfort.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with strap pull tests, elastic stretch/recovery, cup retention checks, and shade review.

Sourcing

Certified mills for poly/spandex and mesh; OEKO-TEX elastics and pads; trims and labels from audited suppliers; all materials tracked for compliance.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 750 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 2,500+ pcs reduce unit cost and secure line time.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.45 - $3.32 including fabric, strappy construction, pads, elastic, flatlock/coverstitch, QC, and packing. Specialty straps or mesh add small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approvals. Rush: 20-22 days for limited colorways with 5-8% expedite.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Hang tags, pad-info inserts, polybags with barcodes/ASIN, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight strappy design, removable pads, and wicking performance.
