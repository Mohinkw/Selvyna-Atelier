Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define armhole depth, neck shape, back width, binding spec, GSM, and length grades. Samples in 8-10 days with options for curved hems and different fabric weights.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 95% Polyester / 5% Spandex (170-190 GSM) or cotton blends for natural handfeel; wicking finish optional.

Cut & Sew: Precision cutting to keep grain straight; racerback shape maintained across sizes; bindings cut on bias for smooth curves.

Finishing: Coverstitch at hems for stretch; soft binding to prevent armhole chafe; label placements planned to avoid irritation.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with seam strength pulls, shade review, dimensional checks, and handfeel inspection.

Sourcing

Poly/spandex or cotton blends from certified mills; soft bindings and threads from compliant suppliers; labels and trims sourced with traceability.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 750 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 2,500+ pcs improve FOB and scheduling.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $1.92 - $2.62 including fabric, binding, coverstitch, QC, and packing. Wicking finish or heavier GSM adds modest premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 19-25 days after approvals. Rush: 18-20 days for focused colorways.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Fold-and-pack polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight wicking, softness, and fit.
