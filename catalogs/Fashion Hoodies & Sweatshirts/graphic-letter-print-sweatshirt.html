Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail graphic size/placement, print technique, GSM, body/sleeve length, rib widths, and shrink allowances. Print approvals and fit's in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% poly french terry (280 GSM) for comfort and print hold.

Printing: Screen print, discharge, or DTG per artwork; flash cure controls; curing to cure spec for durability.

Construction: Cut and sewn with linked shoulders; crewneck rib; rib cuffs/hem; seams overlocked or topstitched.

Finishing: Final QC for print adhesion, color match, and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant terry mills; eco-inks available; buttons/trims nickel-safe; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per design/color.

Price Transparency

Production Time

Bulk: 20-23 days after approvals. Print setup/approvals add 2-3 days.
