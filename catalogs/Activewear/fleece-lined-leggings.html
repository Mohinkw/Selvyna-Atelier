Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shell GSM, brush depth, waistband height, inseam lengths, and stitch specs. Samples in 10-12 days with waistband tension options and opacity checks.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 92% Polyester / 8% Spandex with brushed fleece back (300-320 GSM) for warmth and stretch. Opacity and pill testing performed pre-production.

Cut & Sew: Grain-aligned cutting to prevent twisting; gusset optional for mobility; flatlock seams to minimize bulk.

Waistband: Non-roll elastic or bonded waist; tension tuned per size; optional drawcord for secure fit.

Finishing: Coverstitch hems for stretch recovery; heat setting to stabilize length and reduce shrink.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with stretch recovery, brush consistency, seam strength, shade review, and squat/opacity testing.

Sourcing

Thermal poly/spandex knit's from audited mills; elastics, threads, and labels from compliant suppliers; packaging sourced with sustainability options.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 500 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 1,800+ pcs sharpen FOB.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.85 - $4.72 including brushed fabric, flatlock/coverstitch, waistband, QC, and packing. Bonded waist or gusset adds small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 25-30 days after approval. Rush: 22-24 days on core colors with available fabric.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Fold-pack polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight thermal warmth, brushed interior, and squat-proof opacity.
