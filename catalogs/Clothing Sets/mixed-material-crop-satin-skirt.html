Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify crop top neckline, chest width, crop length; skirt waist, rise, length, and seam placement. Fit'samples in 13-15 days.

Making Process

Fabrics: 95% cotton / 5% spandex jersey (200 GSM) for crop; 97% polyester / 3% spandex satin (140 GSM) for skirt—contrasting textures engineered intentionally.

Coordination: Cotton crop and satin skirt paired for textural contrast within color-coordinated palette.

Crop Top: Fitted fit; finished neckline; quality seaming throughout.

Skirt: Elegant satin drape; quality waistband; finished hem with soft sheen.

Finishing: Careful press on both pieces, respecting each fabric's characteristics; quality closures; full lining on skirt; QC for contrast effect and fit.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton-spandex mills (crop); compliant satin mills (skirt); coordinated color palette verified; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 325 sets per color palette.

Price Transparency

Production Time
