Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify length, shoulder width, sleeve length, belt/tie type (self-tie or elastic), placket depth, and pattern or embellishment options. Fit'samples in 13-15 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% rayon satin (120 GSM) for luxurious hand feel and elegant drape.

Construction: Loose-fitting robe design with self-tie or elastic belt; wide sleeves typical of kimono style.

Pattern Placement: Centered pattern placement or custom design per spec; embellishments like embroidery or beading if applicable.

Belt/Tie: Self-tie sash or elastic belt per design; secure attachment.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining satin sheen; quality binding on placket and neckline; topstitched hems; QC for pattern alignment and belt attachment.

Sourcing

Compliant rayon satin mills; quality thread for topstitching; embellishment materials if needed; labels within RSL.
