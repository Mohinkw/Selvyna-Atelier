Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, inseam length, sleeve length, neckline style, crotch gusset detail, and stretch recovery targets. Fit'samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 92% polyester / 8% spandex (240 GSM) with 4-way stretch and moisture-wicking properties.

Construction: Seamless or flatlock seams minimizing friction; reinforced gusset for movement.

Neckline: Crew neck, V-neck, or mock neck per design; adjustable or fixed.

Sleeves: Long sleeves, short sleeves, or sleeveless per design options.

Finishing: Stretch recovery testing; moisture-wicking validation; QC for seamlessness and stretch performance.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester-spandex athletic mills; moisture-wicking treatments; quality elastic; labels within RSL.
