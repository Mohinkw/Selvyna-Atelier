Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify body length, armhole and neckline shapes, paneling, built-in short length, gusset, and stitch specs. Samples in 10-12 days with liner fabric options and strap/neckline variations.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 92% Polyester / 8% Spandex (240-260 GSM) with moisture-wicking; optional UPF finishes. Liners use breathable nylon/spandex for no-show coverage.

Pattern & Paneling: Princess seams or side panels contour the body; gusseted shorts for mobility; silicone-free stays available for hem security.

Construction: Clean binding on neck/armholes; flatlock or coverstitch seams for comfort; hidden pocket options at shorts.

Finishing: Coverstitched hems or bonded finishes; optional reflective hit's for athleisure crossover.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with stretch/recovery tests, seam strength, shade review, and movement fit checks.

Sourcing

Performance knit's from audited mills; breathable liners; elastics and trims from compliant suppliers; reflective and print options available with colorfast testing.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 500 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 1,800+ pcs improve FOB.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.55 - $5.95 including shell, built-in shorts, gusset, bindings, QC, and packing. UPF or reflective trims add small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 25-30 days after approval. Rush: 22-25 days with limited trims/colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Hang tags, polybags with barcode/ASIN, size stickers, care labels. Messaging can highlight built-in shorts, wicking, and UV/comfort features.
