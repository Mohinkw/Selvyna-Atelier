Tech Pack & Sampling

Making Process

Fabric: 92% polyester / 8% spandex (180 GSM) for stretch, comfort, and moisture-wicking properties.

Construction: Seamless or flat-seamed tube construction for comfortable fit.

Stretch Properties: Engineered stretch to stay in place during activity without slipping.

Options: Available with or without non-slip gripping; single or multi-packs per spec.

Finishing: Quality stitching; comfortable seams; QC for stretch consistency and comfort.
