Premium Mesh Bodysuit Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify chest width, body length, sleeve length, mesh gauge (hole size), neckline style, gusset coverage (options: opaque or mesh), and snap placement. Fit'samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 85% nylon / 15% spandex mesh (150 GSM) with engineered hole pattern for contemporary look.

Mesh Construction: Quality mesh with consistent hole size and open appearance; breathable throughout.

Fit: Comfortable fit through body; mesh naturally drapes without clinging.

Gusset: Options for opaque gusset (quality cotton-blend) or mesh matching body per design spec.

Closure: Quality snaps or buttons at crotch.

Finishing: Careful seaming to prevent running; quality binding on neckline and sleeves; QC for mesh uniformity and closure.

Sourcing

Compliant nylon-spandex mesh mills with consistent pattern; quality opaque gusset fabric or mesh; quality snaps; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.15 - $4.20 including nylon-spandex mesh, opaque or mesh gusset per spec, quality seaming to prevent runs, quality binding, snaps/buttons, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, tissue protection, care tags. Hang tags with sizing and mesh care instructions including run-prevention care tips.
