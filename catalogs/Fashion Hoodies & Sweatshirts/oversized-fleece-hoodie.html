Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define GSM, body/sleeve length, hood drop, drawcord diameter, pocket depth, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days with hang and comfort tests.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% poly fleece (320 GSM) for warmth and breathability.

Construction: Cut and sewn with linked shoulders; reinforced pocket openings; stable hood with drawcord locks; bound or overlocked seams.

Finishing: Shrink control wash; steam to shape; QC for pocket strength, hood symmetry, and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant fleece mills; drawcord with secure ends; pocket reinforcements; trims within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $6.38 - $7.88 including fabric, lining (if any), hood, drawcord, pockets, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Fold strategy protects hood; optional tissue.
