Premium Tiered Mini Skirt Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist, number of tiers, tier width, mini length, waistband type, and shrink allowances. Fit and tier samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton poplin (110 GSM) for structured tiers with body.

Tiers: Multiple fabric layers creating cascading tiered effect; each tier sewn separately per spec; reinforced tier stitching.

Fit: High-waist or mid-waist per spec; relaxed through tiers; mini length.

Construction: Elasticated or zip back waist; side seams; tiered application.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash; gentle press; QC for tier consistency and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton poplin mills; quality elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.20 - $5.25 including cotton poplin, tiered construction, elastic, tier stitching labor, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals. Tier assembly adds 2-3 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Fold protects tiers; optional tissue.
