Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define neckline, sleeve shape, peplum height, flare ratio, waist seam finishes, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to validate waist position and drape.

Making Process

Fabric: 120-140 GSM poly crepe or light twill for structure with flow.

Cutting: Grain-true cutting for hang; dart/waist notch alignment.

Construction: Clean neck facings or binding; stable waist seams; overlock + topstitch or clean finish inside.

Finishing: Press to set peplum flare; QC for waist seam flatness and flare symmetry.

Sourcing

Crepes from compliant mills; interlinings with controlled shrink; buttons or zips nickel-safe; dyes meeting RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color. Sleeve variations can share main body.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.12 - $5.62 including fabric, waist seam labor, facings, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals. Embellishments add 3-5 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding protects neckline and peplum flare.
