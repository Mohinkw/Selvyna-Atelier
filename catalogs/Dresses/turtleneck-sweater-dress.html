Premium Turtleneck Sweater Dress Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify yarn blend, knit gauge, body length, neck height, rib width, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 12-14 days with recovery tests.

Making Process

Yarn & Knit: Cotton/acrylic or viscose/nylon blends (280-320 GSM) for softness and resilience. Gauge balanced for drape and warmth.

Panel Work: Shoulder stabilization tape; linked seams or flatlock where specified; rib trims for cuffs and hem.

Neck Stability: Double-layer turtleneck with light elastic in seam if required to prevent collapse.

Finishing: Steam blocking to measurements; QC for neck recovery, sleeve length, and pilling resistance.

Sourcing

Certified yarn suppliers; anti-pilling finishes on request; compliant labels and trims for winter programs.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color. Yarn-dyed lots can be shade-banded.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $8.80 - $11.20 including yarn, knitting, linking, rib trims, QC, and packing. Anti-pilling or special stitches add premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 28-32 days after approvals. Yarn-dye may add 5 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Folded and bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Neck support fold to keep collar crisp; optional tissue for premium sets.
