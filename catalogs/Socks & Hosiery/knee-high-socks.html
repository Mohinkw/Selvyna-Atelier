Knee-high socks require careful documentation to ensure consistent sizing and stay-up performance. We prepare comprehensive tech packs that include detailed size charts with calf/knee measurements, rib tension specifications, length targets (to prevent twisting), reinforcement zone details, care protocols, and complete packaging requirements. Our documentation is validated against industry standards so every pair fits as intended and stays in place through daily wear.

Making Process

Knee-high socks balance fashion with function. Our manufacturing process:

  • Yarn Blending: Cotton for breathability, polyester for durability, and spandex for sustained stretch through wear and washing.
  • Graduated Knit Density: Calf and knee areas receive lighter knits for comfort; heel and toe zones are reinforced for durability.
  • Engineered Rib Cuffs: Top rib is tuned to stay in place without cutting off circulation�a critical balance for all-day comfort.
  • Length Precision: Each size is knit to exact length specifications; boarding and heat-setting lock in length to prevent post-wash stretching or shrinking.
  • Reinforced Heel/Toe: Extra yarn passes create cushioning and durability in high-wear areas.
  • Linking & Seaming: Flat seams minimize pressure points on shins and calves.
  • Shape-Locking Boarding: Heat treatment prevents twisting and maintains consistent shape through repeated washes.
  • Final QC: 100% pair-matching, length verification, rib tension checks, and visual inspection.
