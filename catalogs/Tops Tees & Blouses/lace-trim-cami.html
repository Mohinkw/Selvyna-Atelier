Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify fabric GSM, lace type/width, strap adjusters, lining options, and shrink allowances. Fit and handfeel samples in 9-11 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 140-160 GSM rayon/spandex jersey for drape and softness.

Lace: Soft galloon or flat lace; scallop alignment at neck; bartack at joins.

Construction: Clean neckline binding; adjustable straps with nickel-safe rings/sliders; optional shelf bra lining.

Finishing: Steam to shape; QC for lace symmetry, strap security, and shade.

Sourcing

Compliant jersey mills; certified lace suppliers; nickel-safe hardware; dyes meeting RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 600 pcs per color. Shared base fabric across lace colors possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.62 - $3.75 including fabric, lace, hardware, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-21 days after approvals. Lace dye lots may add 2-3 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding protects lace scallops; tissue optional.
