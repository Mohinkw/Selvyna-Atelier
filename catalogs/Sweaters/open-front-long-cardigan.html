Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define yarn blend, gauge, body length, pocket type, hem stabilization, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 80% acrylic / 20% poly at 8-9 gauge for soft drape.

Construction: Linked shoulders; reinforced pocket openings; clean front edges with full needle rib or faced edges.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for hem stability, pocket strength, and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant yarn mills; pocket reinforcements; trims within RSL; optional anti-pilling finish.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
