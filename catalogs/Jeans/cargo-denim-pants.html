Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify rise, waist/hip/thigh/knee/leg opening measurements, cargo pocket placement/size, inseam, button/rivet spec, and shrink allowances. Fit and pocket function tests in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 98% cotton / 2% spandex 12oz denim (360 GSM) for durability and minimal stretch.

Cargo Pockets: Multiple leg pockets with flap closures; reinforced pocket openings; sturdy webbing.

Construction: Straight leg; five-pocket foundation plus cargo pockets; reinforced rivet placement; YKK button; heavy topstitching.

Finishing: Controlled wash; pocket seam reinforcement; shrink control; QC for pocket placement accuracy and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant denim mills; rust-free rivets; YKK zippers and buttons; webbing for pocket flaps; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per wash.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.88 - $9.75 including denim, multi-pocket construction, cargo pockets, hardware, wash, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 27-31 days after approvals. Multiple pocket construction and wash add 7-10 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging
