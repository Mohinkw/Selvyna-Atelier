Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail sleeve volume, elastic channel width, neck shape, bust darts, facings, and sweep. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to validate sleeve balance and A-line hang.

Making Process

Fabric: Cotton poplin, viscose twill, or stretch blend (140-180 GSM) for shape retention.

Cutting: Grain-true cutting to maintain sleeve head shape; optional print matching on yokes.

Construction: Clean-finished facings, stay-stitched necklines, reinforced shoulder seams; elastics inserted with controlled tension.

Finishing: Sleeve press to set volume; QC for sleeve symmetry, neck flatness, and sweep.

Sourcing

Poplins and twills from compliant mills; elastics and interfacing tested for recovery and comfort; trims nickel-safe.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color. Mix of sleeve lengths allowed with shared body.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $6.56 - $8.32 including fabric, elastic channels, clean facings, QC, and packing. Prints or embroidery add premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 24-28 days after approvals. Embellishments may add 3-5 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and optional insert card. Folding keeps sleeves uncrushed; tissue optional.
