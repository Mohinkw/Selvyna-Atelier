Premium Kimono Cover-Up Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify kimono length, sleeve length, gauze weight, print/embroidery (if any), neckline style, and shrink allowances. Fit and embroidery samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton gauze (100-120 GSM) for lightweight breathability.

Construction: Relaxed kimono cut per spec; front opening; flutter or bell sleeves per design.

Details: Optional embroidery, prints, or fringe trim per design; edge finishing per style.

Finishing: Shrink control wash; gentle press maintaining relaxed drape; QC for measurements and edge finish quality.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton gauze mills; optional embroidery threads; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color/style.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $4.90 - $6.65 including cotton gauze, construction, optional embroidery/trim, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approvals. Optional embroidery adds 2-3 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, tissue protection, care tags. Hang tags with care instructions for gauze.
