Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs outline yarn blend, gauge, body length, rib widths, armhole depth, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 9-11 days.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 85% acrylic / 15% poly at 9-10 gauge for light layering weight.

Construction: V-neck ribs tuned to lay flat; linked shoulders; rib hem for shape.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for rib tension, measurements, and pilling.

Sourcing

Compliant yarn suppliers; trims/labels within RSL; optional anti-pilling finish.

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color.
