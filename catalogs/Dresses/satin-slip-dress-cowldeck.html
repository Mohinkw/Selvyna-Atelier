Premium Satin Slip Dress (Cowldeck) Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs cover bias angle, cowl depth, strap width, lining choice, hem flare, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 10-12 days to validate drape and coverage.

Making Process

Fabric: 80-120 GSM satin (poly/viscose) with soft wash to reduce cling.

Cutting: True bias cutting; careful nesting to control twist; cowl stabilized at side seams.

Construction: Rolled hems, French seams or clean overlock; bartack at straps and cowl anchoring points.

Finishing: Form steaming to set cowl; QC for neck symmetry, side hang, and shine uniformity.

Sourcing

Satin from compliant mills; nickel-safe rings/sliders if used; dyes tested for colorfastness and crocking.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color. Shared base fabric across lengths possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.20 - $8.96 including fabric, bias cutting, cowl forming, QC, and packing. Specialty washes add $0.30 - $0.60.

Production Time

Bulk: 22-26 days after approvals. Rush on repeat colors feasible.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding avoids cowl creases; tissue optional.
