Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify top measurements (chest, length, sleeve), bottom measurements (waist, length, rise), neckline, and drawstring placement. Fit samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% polyester French terry (280 GSM) for softness and durability.

Coordination: Top and bottom matched in color and fabric for cohesive loungewear aesthetic.

Top: Relaxed fit through shoulders and body; soft fleece lining for comfort.

Bottoms: Comfortable waist with drawstring or elastic; relaxed leg; finished cuffs or wide hem.

Finishing: Soft press without breaking Terry pile; quality stitching; QC for comfort and durability.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton-polyester French terry mills; drawstring or elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 sets per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
