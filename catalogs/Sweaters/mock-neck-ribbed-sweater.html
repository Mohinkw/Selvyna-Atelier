Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify yarn blend, rib width, gauge, neck height, body length, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 10-12 days with recovery and pilling tests.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: Cotton/acrylic ribs at 9-11 gauge for clean vertical lines and comfort.

Collar Stability: Mock neck with tuned rib tension to stay upright without constricting.

Construction: Linked shoulders; rib cuffs/hem; optional full-needle rib neck for stability.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for rib recovery, measurements, and shade.

Sourcing

Compliant yarn suppliers; trims/labels within RSL; anti-pilling finish on request.
