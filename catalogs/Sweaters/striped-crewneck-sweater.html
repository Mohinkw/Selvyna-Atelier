Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail stripe layout, yarn colors, gauge, neck rib width, cuff/hem ribs, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 10-12 days with wash and pilling tests.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 80% acrylic / 20% poly at 9-10 gauge for smooth face and comfort.

Intarsia/Stripe Control: Tight float management to prevent show-through; balanced tension for flat stripes.

Rib Stability: Ribbed neck, cuffs, hem with tuned ratios to avoid grow-out.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for stripe alignment, shade, and measurements.

Sourcing

Colorfast yarns from compliant mills; trims within RSL; optional anti-pilling finish.

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per pattern/color.
