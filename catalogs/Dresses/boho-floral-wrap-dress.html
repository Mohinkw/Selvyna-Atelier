Premium Boho Floral Wrap Dress Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define wrap angle, tie length, modesty panel options, sleeve shape, and sweep. Fit and movement tests in 10-12 days to confirm coverage and waist hold.

Making Process

Fabric: Viscose challis or poly crepe (120-140 GSM) for drape; pre-wash to stabilize.

Cutting: Print matching on bodice and skirt panels; true grain to prevent twist.

Construction: Reinforced tie points; clean finished seams; optional modesty snap; edge stitch for crisp wrap.

Quality Checks: Tie pull tests, wrap overlap measurement, print alignment, and AQL Level 2 inspection.

Sourcing

Printed challis from certified mills; AZO-free dyes; nickel-safe snaps; compliant labels and trims.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per print/color. Multi-print packs can share silhouette.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.20 - $9.20 including fabric, print matching, reinforced ties, QC, and packing. Specialty prints add $0.40 - $0.70.

Production Time

Bulk: 26-30 days after approvals. Printed fabrics add 5-7 days for strike-offs.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folded to protect wrap crease; tissue optional.
