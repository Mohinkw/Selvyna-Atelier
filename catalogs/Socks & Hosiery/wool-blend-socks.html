Making Process

Yarn Selection & Blending: We source premium wool varieties (merino, cashmere, alpaca blends) combined with acrylic and spandex for warmth without itch, durability, and easy care. Yarns are inspected for color consistency, strength, and elasticity before knitting.

Knitting & Reinforcement: Advanced knitting machines create precise knit densities for thermal regulation. Toe and heel reinforcement uses nylon or polyester for durability. Rib cuffs are engineered for gentle compression and reliable stay-up fit without cutting off circulation.

Boarding & Heat Setting: Steam-based boarding stabilizes shape, reduces shrink, and improves handfeel. Heat-setting locks in dimensions for consistent sizing across the production run. Boarding intensity is customized per yarn blend to preserve wool softness while achieving dimensional stability.

Quality Inspections: AQL Level 2 inspections check for pilling (pillow tested at wear points), colorfastness (wash/light testing), seam strength, and handfeel consistency. Every pair is checked for correct fiber composition and weight.
