Making Process

Fabric: 90% acrylic / 10% spandex knit (220 GSM) for warmth and stretch comfort.

Construction: Seamless or flat-seamed tube knit with reinforced thumb holes.

Finger Opening: Open finger area with finished edge (hemmed, cuffed, or rolled per spec).

Finishing: Soft cozy finish; quality thumb reinforcement; comfortable cuffs at wrist; QC for hole placement and edge quality.
