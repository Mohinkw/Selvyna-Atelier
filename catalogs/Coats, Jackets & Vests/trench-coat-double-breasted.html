Making Process

Fabric: 97% polyester / 3% spandex twill (240 GSM) for structure and comfort stretch.

Construction: Tailored fit through shoulders and body; classic length to knee per spec.

Double Breasted: Dual button rows with quality buttons per design; structured front closure.

Details: Notch collar; long sleeves; side slash pockets; optional storm flap; integrated belt with loops.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining structured appearance; topstitched seams; QC for measurements and button durability.
