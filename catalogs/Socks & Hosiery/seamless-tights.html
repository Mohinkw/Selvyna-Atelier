Tech Pack Creation

Seamless tights require specialized tech pack documentation emphasizing seamless knitting parameters, waistband tension, denier targets, opacity standards, gusset design, size charts with fit allowances, wash/care protocols, and packaging specifications. Our documentation ensures seamless construction is maintained across every size and color, delivering the smooth, irritation-free silhouette customers expect.

Sampling

Seamless development samples arrive within 8?10 days, showcasing seamless construction, opacity consistency, waistband comfort, and color accuracy. We evaluate fit, stretch, handfeel, and seamless integrity. Adjustments to waistband height, gusset design, or opacity are iterated quickly with revised samples.

Making Process

Seamless tights deliver exceptional comfort. Our process uses advanced circular knitting machines creating tights without longitudinal seams; double-covered nylon and spandex for enhanced durability and opacity; tuned waistbands preventing rolling while maintaining comfort; color-matched or contrasting gusset options; validated stretch and recovery; ladder-resistance testing; and opacity consistency verified on every batch.

Sourcing

Seamless tights demand premium materials. We source high-quality nylon and spandex yarns optimized for seamless knitting, double-covered yarns for durability, waistband elastics tuned for comfort, gusset materials in multiple colors, luxury dyes, labels, and packaging. Our supply chain is transparent and compliant, supporting your brand's commitment to quality and sustainability.
