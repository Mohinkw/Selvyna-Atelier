Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, blazer length, ruched sleeve details, collar style, button placement, and pocket placement. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% polyester / 5% spandex twill (230 GSM) for structure and stretch comfort.

Fit: Tailored through shoulders and body; fitted through torso; ruched sleeves per design spec.

Ruched Sleeves: Strategic gathering/ruching on sleeves; quality elastic or fabric gathering to maintain shape.

Details: Single or double-breasted button closure per design; notch collar; chest and side pockets.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining ruched detail integrity; reinforced button holes; topstitched seams; QC for sleeve ruching precision.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester twill mills; quality buttons; elastic for ruching if applicable; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.
