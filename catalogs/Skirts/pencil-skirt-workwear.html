Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist, hip/thigh measurements, knee/length, fitted taper spec, waistband type, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 63% polyester / 34% rayon / 3% spandex (220 GSM) for professional structure with minimal stretch.

Fit: High-rise waist; fitted through hip and thigh; tapered toward knee; knee-length or midi per spec.

Construction: Zip back closure; lined front pockets or seam pockets per design; side seams; topstitched.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash; professional press with center crease; QC for measurements and finish.

Sourcing

Compliant mills for professional blends; quality zippers; pocket linings; labels within RSL.
