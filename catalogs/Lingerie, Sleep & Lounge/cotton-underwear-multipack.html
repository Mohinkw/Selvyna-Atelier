Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify pack count (3-7 pieces), styles included (briefs, bikinis, boyshorts), sizes, cotton content, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95-100% cotton with elastane (180-200 GSM) for comfort and durability.

Styles: Mix of styles per pack (briefs, bikinis, boyshorts) or single style per design.

Waistband: Comfortable elastic waistband; quality stitching; tag placement options.

Construction: Reinforced crotch; flatlock or topstitched seams; minimal chafe design.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash; color fastness testing; QC for fit consistency across pack.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton mills; quality elastane; elastic; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per size/style configuration (multipacks counted as unit's).

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.15 - $4.55 per multipack (3-5 pieces) including cotton, elastic, reinforcement, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging
