Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify rib gauge, body length, sleeve shape, neck rib width, GSM, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to validate cling, recovery, and neck stability.

Making Process

Yarn & Fabric: 95% Cotton / 5% Spandex rib (220-240 GSM) or viscose blends for drape. Shade-banded yarn lots to avoid striping.

Cut & Sew: Grain-aligned cutting to keep ribs straight; differential feed to prevent neck stretching; stabilized shoulder seams.

Hems & Neck: Coverstitch hems for stretch; double-needle or binding at neck to prevent grow-out; optional babylock sleeves.

Finishing: Steam and measure to spec; QC for rib alignment, torque, and recovery.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with stretch/recovery tests, shade review, torque checks, and dimensional verification.

Sourcing

Combed cotton and spandex from audited mills; dyes and auxiliaries compliant with RSL; labels and trims sourced with social/environmental certifications.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 1,200+ pcs reduce FOB.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $6.80 - $8.40 including yarn, rib knitting, neck stabilization, coverstitch, QC, and packing. Viscose blends or special dyes add small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 24-28 days after approvals. Rush: 20-22 days on core colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Fold-pack polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight recovery, softness, and easy care.
