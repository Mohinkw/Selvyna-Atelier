About Us

Selvyna Atelier is a premium women’s fashion brand and private label apparel manufacturer in Bangladesh, specializing in high-quality, trend-driven and ethically produced women’s clothing. We offer end-to-end OEM & private label clothing production with low MOQ, covering design, fabric sourcing, pattern development, sampling, bulk manufacturing, packaging, and export logistics.

As a brand, manufacturer and supplier, we deliver refined women’s wear collections while helping emerging and established labels launch and scale through reliable sourcing, premium craftsmanship, and modern manufacturing solutions. With a focus on quality, fit, and responsible production, Selvyna Atelier ensures every garment empowers women with elegance and confidence.
