About Selvyna Atelier

Selvyna Atelier is a women's fashion brand and private label manufacturer based in Bangladesh. We specialize in trend-forward design, ethical production, and factory-direct pricing that helps emerging and established labels bring premium apparel to market quickly.

Who We Are

We blend design studio thinking with disciplined garment manufacturing. From fabric sourcing and pattern development to grading, fitting, sampling, and bulk production, our team delivers consistent quality across seasonal and evergreen lines.

Every collection is supported by transparent lead times, ethical compliance, and responsive communication. Our partners trust us for low-MOQ launches, retail replenishment, and Amazon FBA-ready fulfillment—all under one roof.
