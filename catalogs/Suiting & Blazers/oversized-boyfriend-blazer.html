Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, sleeve length, blazer length, collar style, button placement, and pocket placement. Fit samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% polyester / 5% spandex twill (240 GSM) for structure with comfort stretch.

Fit: Oversized through shoulders and body; dropped shoulder seams per design; comfortable sleeve length.

Details: Single or double-breasted button closure per spec; notch collar; chest and side pockets.

Construction: Reinforced button holes; topstitched seams throughout; quality button hardware.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining structure; quality lining optional; QC for measurements and button durability.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester twill mills; quality buttons; lining fabric optional; labels within RSL.
