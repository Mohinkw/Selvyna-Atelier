Making Process

Fabric: 95% cotton / 5% spandex rib knit (220 GSM) with engineered texture for body-contouring effect.

Rib Construction: Quality rib gauge creating visual texture while maintaining body-hugging fit.

Contoured Fit: Rib knit naturally contours to body; fitted through torso.

Gusset: Quality rib knit gusset matching body ribbing.

Closure: Quality snaps or buttons at crotch; reinforced closure.

Finishing: Soft press without crushing ribs; quality seaming; QC for rib consistency and closure.
