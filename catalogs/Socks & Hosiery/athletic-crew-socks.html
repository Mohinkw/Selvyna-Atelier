Making Process

Athletic crew socks combine performance with comfort, requiring specialized manufacturing:

  • Premium Yarns: We select combed cotton for breathability, polyester for durability, and elastane for consistent stretch and recovery through intense activity and repeated washing.
  • Advanced Knitting: Multi-gauge circular knitting machines create reinforced heel/toe zones and engineered arch compression areas that stay in place during movement.
    • >
  • Moisture Management: Strategic yarn placement maximizes moisture-wicking to keep feet dry during workouts.
  • Precision Linking: Seamless or flat-seam linking minimizes friction points, reducing blistering and improving comfort during high-impact activities.
  • Shape-Setting: Boarding and heat-setting lock in performance features and ensure size stability across the full size run.
  • Colorfast Dyeing: Vibrant, long-lasting colors that wit'stand athletic wear and frequent laundering; AQL checks verify shrinkage, pilling, and seam integrity.
  • Final QC: 100% pair-matching, dimensional verification, and visual inspection before packing and shipping.
