Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, dress length, sleeve length, collar style, button placement, pocket placement, and belt placement (if applicable). Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% polyester / 5% spandex twill (230 GSM) for structure and comfort stretch.

Construction: Tailored shoulders; fitted through bodice; A-line or straight silhouette from hip per spec.

Length: Knee-length or midi per design; classic dress proportions.

Details: Button-down front closure with quality buttons; notch collar; chest and side pockets; optional waist belt.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining structured appearance; reinforced button holes; full lining; topstitched seams; QC for measurements and detail quality.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester twill mills; quality buttons; belt fabric optional; full lining; labels within RSL.
