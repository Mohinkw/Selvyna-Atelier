Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify GSM, body/sleeve length, zip depth, rib widths, zipper spec, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 9-11 days; zipper pull tests included.

Making Process

Fabric: 85% cotton / 15% poly french terry (280 GSM) for softness and durability.

Zipper Placement: Stabilized placket; YKK or equivalent zipper; clean topstitching.

Finishing: Rib cuffs and hem tuned to lie flat; linked shoulders; shrink wash; QC for zip smoothness and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant mills for french terry; brand zippers; trims/labels within RSL.
