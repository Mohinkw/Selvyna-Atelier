Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify length (thigh or knee), sleeve style, neckline, shoulder seam placement, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 9-11 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton jersey or woven (140-160 GSM) for softness and breathability.

Fit: Oversized or relaxed through body; short or long sleeves per spec; simple crew neck or V-neck.

Length: Mid-thigh to knee per design; longer proportions for sleeping comfort.

Construction: Simple seaming; reinforced neck and armholes; topstitched.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash maintaining softness; gentle press; QC for measurements and fabric quality.
