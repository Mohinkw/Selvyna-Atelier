Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify chest width, body length, sleeve length, neckline style, button placement spacing, gusset sizing, and snap placement at crotch. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton poplin (130 GSM) for crisp appearance and everyday comfort.

Construction: Tailored fit with front button placket; collar per spec (pointed, spread, or band collar).

Buttons: Quality buttons spaced per tech pack; reinforced buttonholes throughout placket.

Gusset: Quality gusset construction for comfort through movement.

Closure: Quality snaps or buttons at crotch; hidden snap inside shirt tails.

Finishing: Careful press with crisp creases on front placket; topstitched seams; quality hems; QC for button placement and closure.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton poplin mills; quality buttons; quality snaps; labels within RSL.
