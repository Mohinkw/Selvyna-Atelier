Making Process

Compression socks combine technical precision with therapeutic benefit. Our manufacturing process:

  • Technical Yarn Selection: High-quality nylon and premium spandex blends engineered for sustained compression without loss of elasticity.
  • Fine-Gauge Knitting: Specialized machines create graduated compression, with higher density at the ankle, tapering toward the calf for optimal blood circulation support.
  • Mapped Zones: Each sock has defined compression zones?ankle (highest), arch, and calf?tuned to deliver therapeutic benefit without discomfort.
  • Reinforced Toe & Heel: Flat-seam construction and extra reinforcement prevent blistering and pressure points during extended wear.
  • Pressure Testing: Every batch undergoes pressure-gauge testing to confirm mmHg ranges per size; data is logged for your records.
  • Boarding for Retention: Heat-boarding locks compression into the fiber structure so it resists degradation through multiple washes and wears.
  • Final Certification: QA reports with photos, compression measurements, and size verification before shipment.
