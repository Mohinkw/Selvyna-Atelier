Our Services

End-to-End Fashion Manufacturing & Sourcing Solutions

Design & Pre-Production

  • Product Research & Development

    Our R&D team provides the latest insights on market trends, fabric innovation, washing techniques, and finishes to keep your collection competitive and modern.

  • Technical Design & Tech Packs

    Expert designers create comprehensive, factory-ready Tech Packs including detailed measurements, construction notes, material specifications, and print/embroidery placement for flawless execution.

  • Pattern Making & Sampling

    We develop precise patterns and handle multiple rounds of sample production to ensure perfect fit, design validation, and material integrity before moving to bulk manufacturing.

Manufacturing & Quality Assurance

  • Bulk Production Management

    We oversee all aspects of cut-and-sew, stitching, garment dyeing, and finishing. Our systems support flexible MOQs (Minimum Order Quantities) to accommodate both small-batch and high-volume orders.

  • Specialized Production

    Expertise in various textile categories, including Knitwear (sweaters, tops), Woven (dresses, trousers, shirts), and specialized wash/finishing services.

  • Quality Assurance (QA/QC)

    Our dedicated Quality Control teams conduct rigorous multi-stage inspections (raw material, in-line, and final) based on AQL standards to ensure every unit meets your exact specifications and international quality benchmarks.

  • Compliance & Ethical Sourcing

    We ensure strict adherence to BSCI, Sedex, and other international labor and environmental standards across all production unit's, providing you with a transparent and ethical supply chain.

Supply Chain & Logistics Management

  • Material Sourcing & Negotiation

    Utilizing our vast network, we source and select the highest-quality yarns, fabrics (including sustainable options), and trims, while negotiating competitive pricing on your behalf.

  • Merchandising & Status Reporting

    Our Merchandising team manages communication, production timelines, and documentation, providing regular status reports so you always know the exact progress of your order.

  • Private Label & Branding

    Complete private label services, including custom branding, labeling, packaging, barcoding, and Amazon FBA Prep to get your products market-ready immediately.

  • Global Shipping & Logistics

    We manage the complete supply chain logistics, including loading supervision, customs clearance, and global shipping, ensuring reliable, on-time delivery to your destination.
