Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist/hip/thigh/leg opening measurements, cargo pocket placement/size, inseam, waistband/drawstring style, and shrink allowances. Fit and pocket tests in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% nylon ripstop (120 GSM) for durability and light weight.

Cargo Pockets: Multiple side and thigh pockets with flap closures; reinforced pocket stitching; webbing or trim details.

Fit: Relaxed through hip and thigh; tapered or straight leg per spec; parachute proportions.

Construction: Drawstring or elastic waist; multiple pockets; heavy topstitching; reinforced stress points.

Finishing: Light wash or color setting; QC for pocket placement accuracy and seam strength.

Sourcing

Compliant ripstop nylon mills; quality elastic; webbing; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.
