Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify off-shoulder neckline depth, shoulder seam placement, sleeve length, inseam length, shorts rise, and print placement. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% rayon challis (110 GSM) for soft drape and breezy feel.

Neckline: Off-shoulder design with elastic casing or ruffle detail per spec; comfortable shoulder coverage.

Sleeves: Cap sleeves, flutter sleeves, or sleeveless per design.

Fit: Fitted through torso; relaxed shorts leg; comfortable inseam.

Finishing: Gentle wash preserving challis softness; print application per design (if applicable); QC for fit accuracy and print quality.

Sourcing

Compliant rayon challis mills; quality elastic; print suppliers; labels within RSL.
