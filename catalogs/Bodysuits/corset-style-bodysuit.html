Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify chest width, body length, boning placement (typical 7-11 vertical bones), bone type and strength, neckline style, closure type (busk, hooks & eyes, or lacing points), gusset sizing, and snap placement. Fit'samples in 13-15 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% nylon / 20% spandex with interior boning (240 GSM) for structured silhouette and support.

Boning Structure: Vertical bones placed per tech pack; quality plastic or steel spiral bones ensuring shape retention and comfort.

Construction: Reinforced bone channels created with care to prevent bone poking; quality topstitching throughout.

Closure: Professional closure per spec (busk front, hooks & eyes, or lacing points); quality hardware if metal closures used.

Gusset: Quality gusset construction for comfort despite structured design.

Finishing: Careful press respecting boning structure; quality binding on neckline; reinforced hems; QC for bone alignment and closure quality.

Sourcing

Compliant nylon-spandex mills; quality plastic or steel spiral bones; professional boning hardware; quality gusset fabric; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time

Bulk: 27-31 days after approvals. Boning insertion and testing add time.
