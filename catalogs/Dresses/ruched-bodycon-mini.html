Premium Ruched Bodycon Mini Dress Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define fabric GSM, lining, ruching panel width, elastic channels, neck shape, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days with stretch and recovery tests.

Making Process

Fabric: 220-260 GSM poly/spandex or nylon/spandex with high recovery; optional mesh overlay for texture.

Cut & Sew: Grain-aligned cutting; ruching channels with controlled tension; overlock + coverstitch for stretch seams.

Lining: Lightweight tricot or power mesh to smooth silhouette; bartack at stress points.

Finishing: Even gather distribution checked; QC for stretch/recovery, seam strength, and shade.

Sourcing

Spandex blends from compliant mills; colorfastness, stretch, and pilling tested; trims and elastics nickel-safe.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color. Mixed necklines can share base fabric.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $6.88 - $8.64 including fabric, ruching labor, lining, QC, and packing. Mesh overlay adds $0.40 - $0.70.

Production Time

Bulk: 22-26 days after approvals. Rush on core colors feasible.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding minimizes crush on ruching; optional tissue.
