Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify blazer specifications (shoulder, chest, length, sleeves, collar, buttons, pockets) and pants specifications (waist, inseam, rise, pockets, closure). Fit'samples in 13-15 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 63% polyester / 34% rayon / 3% spandex (220 GSM) for elegant drape and professional appearance.

Blazer: Tailored fit with structured shoulders; notch or peak collar per design; button closure with quality hardware.

Pants: Tailored fit matching blazer proportion; flat-front or pleated per spec; quality zipper closure; structured waistband.

Coordination: Both pieces match in fabric, color, and quality ensuring cohesive professional appearance.

Finishing: Careful press on both pieces; full lining on blazer; reinforced seams; QC for matching measurements and coordinated quality.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester-rayon mills; quality buttons and zippers; lining fabric; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per suit'set.
