Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define GSM, neck shape, sleeve length, sweep, shrink allowances, and label placement. Fit samples in 8-10 days to verify drape and sweep.

Making Process

Fabric: 180-200 GSM combed cotton jersey or rayon/spandex for extra drape; pre-wash to manage shrink.

Cut & Sew: Grain-true cutting to avoid torque; coverstitch hems; taped shoulders for stability.

Necklines: Rib or self-binding; stay-stitch to prevent grow-out; twin-needle finish for flat lay.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with torque, shrink, and shade audits; drape and sweep measurement.

Sourcing

Combed cotton and rayon blends from audited mills; eco dyes and softeners; trims sourced with compliance documentation.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color. Mixed sleeve lengths possible using shared body.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $5.52 - $6.72 including fabric, coverstitch, taped shoulders, QC, and packing. Rayon blends add $0.30 - $0.60.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-24 days after approvals. Faster repeats on carryover colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert cards. Fold to protect neck shape; optional tissue for premium sets.

Selvyna Atelier Advantage

  • Neck Stability: Taped shoulders and stay-stitching to prevent ripples.
  • Smooth Drape: Controlled GSM and shrink to keep swing consistent.
  • Color Reliability: Shade-banded lots to reduce variance.
  • QC Transparency: Torque, shrink, and sweep data shared per run.
