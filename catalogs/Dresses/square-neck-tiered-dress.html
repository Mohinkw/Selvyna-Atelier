Premium Square Neck Tiered Dress Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail tier heights, gather ratios, neck width/height, sleeve shape, lining options, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to confirm balance and coverage.

Making Process

Fabric: Rayon challis, cotton voile, or light twill (120-150 GSM) for floaty tiers.

Cutting: True grain cutting for even hang; tier panels pre-marked for gather ratios.

Construction: Clean-finished facings or binding at neck; evenly distributed gathers; optional lining on light colors.

Finishing: Light steam to set tiers; QC for gather balance, neck symmetry, and sweep.

Sourcing

Certified mills for challis/voile; AZO-free dyes; compliant trims and labels.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color. Multi-tier color-blocking available with shared base.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.04 - $8.72 including fabric, tier gather labor, QC, and packing. Lining or embroidery adds $0.40 - $0.80.

Production Time

Bulk: 24-28 days after approvals. Prints add time for strike-offs.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Fold to protect neckline and tier volume; tissue optional.
