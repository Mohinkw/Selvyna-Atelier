Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify rise, waist/hip/thigh/knee/leg opening measurements, pleat depth/placement, inseam, pocket style, waistband type, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 97% polyester / 3% spandex twill (220 GSM) for lightweight structure and slight stretch.

Pleats: Sharp front pleats with careful placement; press set for longevity; reinforced pleat stitching.

Construction: Lined front pockets; zip fly with button waistband; side seams; clean topstitching.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash; press to shape with pleat sets; QC for pleat sharpness and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant twill mills; quality zippers; buttons; pocket linings; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
