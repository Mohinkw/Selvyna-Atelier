Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify width, length, print style, color palette, fringe/edge detail (if any), and shrink allowances. Print approvals and fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% rayon crepe or challis (120 GSM) for drape and fluidity.

Print: Tropical, botanical, or ethnic prints applied via digital or screen per spec; vibrant colors with good fastness.

Edge Finishing: Hemmed or fringed edges per design; reinforced for durability in beach environment.

Dimensions: Rectangular sarong cut per standard or custom sizing; wrap-style with open ends.

Finishing: Shrink control wash; edge finishing quality; QC for print consistency and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant rayon mills; quality print inks; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per print.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.15 - $4.55 including rayon, tropical print, edge finishing, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approvals. Print setup adds 2-3 days.
