Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify top measurements (chest, length, sleeve), bottom measurements (waist, inseam, rise, length), neckline, closures, and pocket placement. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% cotton / 5% spandex jersey (200 GSM) for comfort and stretch recovery in both pieces.

Coordination: Top and bottom matched in color, pattern, and fabric quality for cohesive appearance.

Top Construction: Fitted or relaxed silhouette per spec; quality seaming throughout.

Bottom Construction: Tailored waist; comfortable rise; finished leg hems or cuffs per design.

Finishing: Careful press on both pieces; topstitched seams; QC for color matching and coordinated fit.

Compliant cotton-spandex jersey mills; color matching verified across both pieces; labels within RSL.
