Premium Batwing Sleeve Pullover Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify yarn blend, gauge, body length, sleeve opening, neck shape, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 10-12 days to validate drape and sleeve comfort.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 80% acrylic / 20% poly at 7-9 gauge for softness and light structure.

Panels: Dolman/batwing shaping knit into body; linked seams for smooth underarm; rib trims at neck and hem.

Finishing: Steam blocking to spec; QC for neck stability, drape, and pilling.

Sourcing

Certified yarn mills; buttons/trims nickel-safe if added; dyes within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $7.50 - $9.75 including yarn, knitting, linking, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Folded and bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Fold strategy keeps neckline flat; optional tissue.

Selvyna Atelier Advantage

  • Stable Necklines: Rib ratios tuned to prevent grow-out.
  • Smooth Underarm: Linked seams for comfort and clean look.
  • Drape Control: Gauge and GSM balanced to avoid collapse.
  • QC Visibility: Pilling and measurement reports per lot.
