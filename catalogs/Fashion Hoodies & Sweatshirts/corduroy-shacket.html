Premium Corduroy Shacket (Shirt-Jacket) Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define wale size, body length, sleeve shape, pocket type, button spec, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 11-13 days with nap direction checks.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton corduroy 8 wale (240 GSM) with soft pile.

Nap Direction: Careful nap alignment in cutting; consistent pile direction across panels.

Tailoring: Clean plackets, reinforced button stands, topstitched seams; optional chest pockets.

Finishing: Light brush to enhance pile; steam to shape; QC for pile uniformity and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton mills; buttons dyed-to-match; interlinings with controlled shrink; trims within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Production Time
