Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, coat length, sleeve length, collar style, button placement (double-breasted), pocket placement, and lining options. Fit samples in 13-15 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 60% wool / 35% polyester / 5% other fibers (400 GSM) for classic warmth and structure.

Construction: Tailored fit with structured shoulders; knee-length proportion per design.

Double Breasted: Dual button rows with quality buttons per design; classic nautical styling.

Details: Notch collar with undercollar; full lining with quality cotton/polyester; inside pockets; side slash pockets.

Finishing: Careful pressing maintaining structure; reinforced buttonholes; topstitched seams; QC for measurements and wool quality.

Sourcing

Compliant wool-blend mills; quality buttons; lining fabric; labels within RSL.
