Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify rise, waist/hip/thigh/knee measurements, cropped inseam, pocket style, button type, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% cotton / 5% spandex twill (200 GSM) for structured feel with comfort stretch.

Capri Length: Cropped inseam per spec; designed to hit mid-calf; clean proportions.

Fit: Mid-rise waist; straight through hip and thigh; classic capri proportions.

Construction: Lined front pockets; zip fly with button waistband; topstitched seams.

Finishing: Light wash or color setting; controlled shrink; QC for inseam accuracy and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant cotton mills; quality zippers; buttons; pocket linings; labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color.
