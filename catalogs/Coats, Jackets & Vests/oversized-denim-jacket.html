Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, sleeve length, jacket length, pocket placement, button/snap closure, and wash finish. Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% cotton denim 12oz (360 GSM) for classic durability and structured appearance.

Fit: Oversized through shoulders and body; length per design; dropped or standard shoulder seam.

Details: Functional chest pockets; side slash pockets; button or snap front closure per spec.

Stitching: Contrast topstitching per design; reinforced seams throughout; rivets at stress points.

Finishing: Controlled wash per spec (vintage, light, medium, or dark); hem stitching; QC for measurements and wash consistency.

Sourcing

Compliant 12oz denim mills; quality buttons or snaps; rivets; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per wash/color.
