Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify size range, length, control panel placement (core, hips, thighs), compression level, neckline, strap style, and gusset detail. Fit'samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 87% nylon / 13% spandex control blend (240 GSM) for smoothing without bulk.

Control Panels: Strategic panel placement targeting core, hip, and thigh areas; graduated compression per design.

Breathability: Mesh or perforated fabric in non-control areas; ventilation zones in armpit's and crotch.

Construction: BodySuit with integrated gusset; adjustable or fixed straps per spec; reinforced seams throughout.

Finishing: Control panel stretch testing; comfort testing under fitted clothing; QC for compression accuracy and fit.

Sourcing

Compliant control-blend nylon-spandex mills; mesh fabrics; quality elastic; labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per size/color.
