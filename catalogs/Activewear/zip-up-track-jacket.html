Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail body length, collar height, zip spec, pocket placements, stitch types, and GSM. Samples in 10-12 days with options for mesh lining, reflective tapes, and cuff/hem treatments.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 100% Polyester Interlock (260-280 GSM) or poly/spandex for stretch; moisture-wicking or brushed back options available.

Cut & Sew: Precision panel cutting to align seams; raglan or set-in sleeves per design. Clean zip insertion with auto-lock pullers.

Pockets & Binding: Zipped welt pockets or concealed side pockets; optional thumbhole cuffs; taped seams for select styles.

Finishing: Coverstitch hems for stretch, optional bonding for modern aesthetics, bar tacks at stress points.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with zip cycle tests, seam strength pulls, shade review, and dimensional checks.

Sourcing

Interlock and mesh from certified mills; YKK or SBS zippers; reflective trims and elastic from audited suppliers; compliant labels and packaging.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 250 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 1,200+ pcs improve FOB and scheduling priority.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $5.25 - $6.65 including fabric, zippers, pocket construction, finishing, QC, and packing. Reflective trims or bonding add modest premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 25-30 days after approval. Rush: 22-25 days with limited trims and colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Hang tags, size stickers, barcoded/ASIN polybags, care labels, and brand neck labels. Messaging can highlight wicking, stretch, and secure pockets.
