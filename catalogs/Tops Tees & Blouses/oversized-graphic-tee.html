Premium Oversized Graphic Tee Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs cover body length, drop shoulder, rib width, graphic size/placement, shrink allowance, and print technique. Lab dips and fit/print samples in 8-10 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 180-200 GSM combed cotton jersey with silicone softener for handfeel; pre-washed for shrink control.

Cut & Sew: Grain-true cutting to avoid torque; taped shoulders for neck stability; coverstitch hems.

Printing: Plastisol, water-based, discharge, or DTG per artwork; flash cure controls to prevent scorch.

Finishing: Steam, measure, and final QC for shade, print adhesion, and torque.

Sourcing

Combed cotton yarn from compliant mills; eco-friendly inks available; trims and labels sourced with social and chemical compliance.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 500 pcs per design/color. Multi-graphic programs can share fabric base.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.62 - $3.75 including fabric, rib, shoulder taping, chosen print, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-21 days after approvals. Complex print setups add 2-4 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, size sticker, hang tag, and optional insert card. Neck print or woven labels available; fold to protect print face.
