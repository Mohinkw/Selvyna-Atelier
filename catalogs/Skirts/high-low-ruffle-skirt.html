Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify front/back length, high-low drop, number of ruffle layers, ruffle width, waist measurements, and shrink allowances. Fit and ruffle samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 100% polyester chiffon (80 GSM) for lightweight drape and romantic movement.

Ruffles: Multiple chiffon layers creating cascading ruffle effect; careful ruffle placement per spec; reinforced ruffle stitching.

High-Low Hem: Asymmetrical high-low cut with short front and longer back per proportions spec.

Construction: Elasticated or zip back waist; side seams; layered ruffle application.

Finishing: Gentle wash; careful press preserving ruffle movement; QC for ruffle consistency and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant chiffon mills; quality elastic; labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color.
