Tech Pack & Sampling

Making Process

Fabric: 190-210 GSM cotton/spandex jersey for comfort and rebound.

Cut & Sew: Grain-true cutting; ribbed or bound neck; elasticized leg openings; snap placket with reinforced stitches.

Snaps: Nickel-safe snaps with pull tests to spec; clean topstitching.

Finishing: Steam and measure; QC for recovery, snap strength, and shade.
