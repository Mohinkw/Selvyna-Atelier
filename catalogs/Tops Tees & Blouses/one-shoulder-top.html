Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define neckline angle, elastic channel width, sleeve options, hem shape, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 9-11 days to validate hold and coverage.

Making Process

Fabric: 180-200 GSM poly/spandex jersey for drape and recovery; pre-wash to stabilize.

Cut & Sew: Grain-true cutting to avoid torque; elasticated neckline with calibrated tension; coverstitch hems.

Finishing: Steam to set shape; QC for elastic hold, side seam hang, and shade.

Sourcing

Compliant jersey suppliers; elastics nickel-safe; dyes and softeners within RSL limit's.
