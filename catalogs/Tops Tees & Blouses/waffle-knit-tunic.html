Premium Waffle Knit Tunic Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waffle size, GSM, neck shape, drop shoulder, side slit's, and shrink allowances. Fit and handfeel samples in 9-11 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 210-230 GSM cotton/spandex waffle for recovery and breathability; pre-washed to control shrink.

Cut & Sew: Grain-aligned cutting; coverstitch hems; taped shoulders for stability; optional curved hem.

Finishing: Light steam to keep waffle loft; QC for shade, torque, and neck flatness.

Sourcing

Waffle knit's from compliant mills; eco softeners; trims and labels sourced with RSL documentation.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color. Shared body across sleeve lengths possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.75 - $4.88 including fabric, coverstitch, shoulder tape, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-23 days after approvals. Melange yarns may add 2-3 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folded to protect neck shape; optional tissue.

Selvyna Atelier Advantage
