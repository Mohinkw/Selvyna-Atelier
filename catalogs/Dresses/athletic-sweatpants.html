Premium Halter Neck Summer Dress Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs capture neck depth, tie length, armhole curve, lining options, and hem shape. Fit'samples in 10-12 days to validate coverage and comfort.

Making Process

Fabric: Rayon challis or poly crepe (110-130 GSM) for airflow; pre-wash to reduce shrink.

Cutting: True grain for clean drape; print panel matching where required.

Construction: Faced or bound neck and armholes; reinforced tie points; optional elastic back for fit ease.

Finishing: Light steam to set hang; QC for armhole comfort, neck symmetry, and sweep.

Sourcing

Certified mills for light wovens; AZO-free dyes; nickel-safe hardware if rings/sliders used.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color/print. Shared body across prints possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $6.32 - $7.84 including fabric, facings/binding, QC, and packing. Prints or embroidery add $0.30 - $0.60.

Production Time

Bulk: 22-26 days after approvals. Print strike-offs add 5-7 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and insert card. Folding protects neckline; optional tissue for premium sets.

Selvyna Atelier Advantage

  • Secure Ties: Reinforced tie points for longevity.
  • Comfort Coverage: Armhole shaping tuned to avoid gaping.
  • Drape Management: Pre-wash and light press to keep airy hang.
  • QC Transparency: Shade, stitch, and sweep reporting per lot.

Why Choose Bangladesh for Summer Dresses?

  • Lightweight Wovens: Strong supply base for challis and crepe.
  • Cost Efficiency: Competitive FOB for warm-weather programs.
  • Print Capability: Reliable print development for florals and geo motifs.
  • Compliance: Socially audited factories for export markets.
