Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define yarn blend, gauge, cable repeat, placket width, button size, rib trims, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 12-14 days with wash and stretch tests.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 100% acrylic or cotton/acrylic at 5-7 gauge for chunky body and softness.

Panels: Cable charts followed on automated flat knitting; shoulder linking for strength; rib cuffs and hem.

Placket: Stabilized placket with interlining where required; reinforced buttonholes; secure buttons.

Finishing: Steam blocking to measurements; QC for cable symmetry, measurements, and pilling.

Sourcing

Certified yarn suppliers; buttons dyed-to-match; interlinings with controlled shrink; trims compliant with RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color. Mixed cable layouts possible with shared yarn.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $9.00 - $11.62 including yarn, knitting, linking, buttons, QC, and packing. Wool blends add premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 27-31 days after approvals. Yarn-dye may add 5 days.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Folded and bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Placket support fold to avoid creasing; optional tissue.
