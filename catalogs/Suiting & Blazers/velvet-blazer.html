Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, blazer length, sleeve length, collar style, button placement, pocket placement, and nap direction (velvet consideration). Fit'samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% polyester / 5% spandex velvet (280 GSM) for luxurious soft feel and appearance.

Construction: Tailored fit with structured shoulders; fitted through body; classic blazer proportions.

Nap Direction: Careful pattern cutting considering velvet nap for consistent color appearance across all pieces.

Details: Single-breasted button closure with quality buttons per spec; notch or peak collar; chest and side pockets.

Finishing: Gentle press using appropriate method for velvet care; careful seam stitching to avoid crushing pile; full lining; QC for nap consistency and finish quality.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester velvet mills; quality buttons; full lining; labels within RSL.
