Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define body length, hem tension, cup depth, strap placement, paneling, and neck/arm binding specs. Samples in 10-12 days with options for built-in shelf bra vs. removable cups and various hem tensions.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 85% Nylon / 15% Spandex (240 GSM) with brushed or sleek face options; optional double-knit for extra coverage.

Support Architecture: Integrated shelf bra, removable pads, and wide underbust elastic stabilized to prevent roll; darting or paneling to contour the bust.

Hem & Binding: Clean coverstitch or bonded hems for comfort; soft binding on neck and armholes to eliminate chafe.

Construction: Flatlock or zigzag seams for stretch; strategic power mesh lining available for added support.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with elastic recovery tests, cup pocket checks, shade review, and seam strength pulls.

Sourcing

Traceable nylon/spandex fabrics, OEKO-TEX elastics, breathable mesh, and certified pads. Labels and trims procured from compliant suppliers with social and environmental audits.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 750 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 2,500+ pcs improve FOB and lead time priority.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.80 - $3.85 including fabric, shelf bra or pad construction, elastics, flatlock/coverstitch, QC, and packing. Mesh lining or bonded hems add modest premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-27 days after approval. Rush: 20-22 days with limited colors and a small expedite.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Branded polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, pad-info inserts, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight longline coverage, smoothing fit, and removable cups.
