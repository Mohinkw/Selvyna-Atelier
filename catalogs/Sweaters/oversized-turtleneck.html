Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify yarn blend, gauge, neck height, rib width, body length, and shrink allowances. Size sets in 12-14 days with recovery and pilling tests.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: Cotton/acrylic ribs at 7-9 gauge for balanced weight and softness.

Collar Stability: Double-layer turtleneck with light elastic option to prevent collapse.

Panels: Linked shoulders; rib cuffs and hem; drop shoulder fit.

Finishing: Steam blocking to spec; QC for neck recovery, measurements, and pilling.

Sourcing

Compliant yarn suppliers; anti-pilling finish on request; trims and labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 300 pcs per color.
