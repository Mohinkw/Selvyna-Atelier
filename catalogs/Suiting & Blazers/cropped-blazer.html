Making Process

Fabric: 97% polyester / 3% spandex crepe (200 GSM) for elegant drape with comfort stretch.

Fit: Tailored through shoulders and body; cropped length per spec; fitted sleeves.

Details: Single or double-breasted button closure per design; structured collar; chest and side pockets.

Construction: Reinforced button holes; topstitched seams; quality button hardware; darts for shaping per design.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining crepe drape; quality lining; QC for measurements and tailoring precision.
