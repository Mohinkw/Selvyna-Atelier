Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs map knit zones, compression levels, rib placements, mesh panels, waistband tension, and bra support tiers. Samples in 12-14 days to validate contour lines, opacity, and fit.

Making Process

Yarn & Knit: 90% Nylon / 10% Spandex seamless knit (200-240 GSM) with engineered mesh/rib zones. Shade-banded yarns to ensure consistent contour color.

Compression Mapping: Zoned compression at waistband and underbust; breathable mesh at heat zones; ribbing for structure without side seams.

Seamless Construction: Tubular knitting eliminates side seams; minimal joining for gussets and crotch; soft waistband elastic inserted or knit-in.

Finishing: Heat setting for dimensional stability; brushing optional for handfeel; coverstitch or bonding at hems when required.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with stretch/recovery tests, opacity/squat tests, waistband tension pulls, and shade review.

Sourcing

Certified nylon/spandex yarns for seamless machines; compliant elastics and pads (if set includes bra); trims and labels from audited suppliers.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 500 sets per color. Volume tiers at 1,800+ sets reduce unit cost.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $5.60 - $7.35 per set (bra + legging/short) including yarn, seamless knitting, compression mapping, heat setting, QC, and packing. Brushed or jacquard effects add small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 27-33 days after approval. Rush: 24-26 days with focused colorways and yarn availability.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Fold-and-pack or set polybags with barcode/ASIN, size stickers, hang tags, and care labels. Messaging can highlight seamless comfort, squat-proof opacity, and contour design.
