Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs detail yarn blend, gauge, collar height, placket width, zipper spec, rib widths, and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days; zipper pull tests included.

Making Process

Yarn & Gauge: 85% acrylic / 15% poly at 9-10 gauge for light warmth and smooth face.

Collar & Zip: Stabilized collar with interlining; YKK or equivalent zipper; clean placket sewing.

Panels: Linked shoulders; rib cuffs and hem; optional contrast tipping.

Finishing: Steam blocking; QC for zip smoothness, measurements, and pilling.

Sourcing

Certified yarn mills; brand zippers; interlinings with controlled shrink; trims within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color.
