Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define GSM, body/sleeve length, hood drop, raw hem width, pocket depth, and shrink allowances. Fit samples in 10-12 days with raw-hem stability checks.

Making Process

Fabric: 80% cotton / 20% poly fleece (300 GSM) for softness and durability.

Raw Hems: Carefully cut raw edges; single-ply for intentional frayed look; edge-stitched or reinforced depending on design.

Construction: Hood with secure drawcord; reinforced pocket openings; linked shoulders; clean seams.

Finishing: Controlled shrink wash; steam; QC for raw hem consistency and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant fleece mills; sturdy drawcord; pocket reinforcements; labels within RSL.

Standard MOQ: 400 pcs per color.
