Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify crossover angle, waist height, inseam lengths (petite/regular/tall), flare opening, stitch specs, and GSM. Samples in 10-12 days with waistband tension options and flare width variants for fit validation.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 92% Polyester / 8% Spandex (260-280 GSM) for drape, opacity, and recovery. Shade control and pill testing before cutting.

Crossover Waist Construction: Stabilized waistband panels cut on-grain to prevent twisting; elastic tuned for stay-put fit without dig-in.

Flare Shaping & Hemming: Precise flare grading per size curve; coverstitch or blind hem options for clean hang and stretch retention.

Boarding & Heat Setting: Steam setting locks flare shape and inseam length, reducing shrink and leg twist.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with stretch recovery, waistband tension pulls, hem stretch tests, and shade inspections under D65.

Sourcing

Certified knit mills for poly/spandex; elastics and threads from audited suppliers; labels and trims sourced with compliance and sustainability review.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 1,200+ pcs unlock sharper FOB and faster line allocation.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $3.50 - $4.55 including fabric, crossover waistband stabilization, flare shaping, boarding, QC, and packing. Specialty dyes or brushed backs add small premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 23-30 days post-approval. Rush: 20-24 days with limited colorways and 5-8% expedite.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Belly bands or fold-and-hang, branded polybags with barcodes/ASIN, size stickers, care labels. Messaging can highlight crossover tummy-control and draped flare.
