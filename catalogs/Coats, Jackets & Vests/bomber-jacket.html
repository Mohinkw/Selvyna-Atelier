Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, jacket length, sleeve length, ribbed cuff widths, waistband height, zipper placement, and pocket placement. Fit'samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Shell Fabric: 100% polyester taffeta (140 GSM) for lightweight, water-resistant exterior.

Lining: Polyester with quilted padding (260 GSM) for light insulation and comfort.

Fit: Relaxed through shoulders and body; tapered with elastic waistband per design.

Details: Full-length zipper with wind flap; ribbed cuffs and collar; zippered side pockets.

Finishing: Quality elastic ribbing; careful press maintaining bomber proportions; QC for measurements and quilting quality.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester mills; quality zippers; elastic ribbing; labels within RSL.
