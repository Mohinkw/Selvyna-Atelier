Making Process

Colorful pattern socks combine technical knitting with creative design. Our process includes:

  • Design Preparation: We collaborate on artwork file setup, ensuring knit-compatible separations for jacquard or intarsia knitting.
  • Yarn Selection: Colorfast cotton, polyester, and elastane blends that hold vibrant hues through multiple washes without fading.
  • Jacquard/Intarsia Knitting: Specialized machines create complex pattern weaves in real time; each color yarn is fed precisely to build intricate designs without compromising structural integrity.
  • Print Alternative: For simpler patterns, direct printing onto pre-knit'socks offers cost advantages and faster turnarounds than intarsia.
  • Reinforced Comfort Zones: Heel and toe receive extra passes and arch support yarn for durability and comfort despite elaborate patterns.
  • Colorfast Processing: Proprietary dye fixation and washing ensure colors remain vibrant and aligned across repeated wears.
  • Quality Verification: AQL inspections check color matching against PMS/samples, motif clarity, seam strength, and stretch retention.

Sourcing
