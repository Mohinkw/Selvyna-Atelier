Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify waist overlap amount, midi length, tie length, waist/hip measurements, slit depth (if any), and shrink allowances. Fit'samples in 10-12 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 55% linen / 45% rayon (140 GSM) for texture and natural drape.

Wrap Construction: Overlapping front panels with tie-waist closure; secure interior closure (button or snap) to prevent slipping.

Fit: Adjustable tie-waist allows sizing flexibility; side seams; optional front or side slit for movement.

Finishing: Shrink control wash; careful press maintaining wrap drape; QC for wrap security and measurements.

Sourcing

Compliant linen-rayon mills; quality buttons or snaps; labels within RSL.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 350 pcs per color.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $5.25 - $6.65 including linen-rayon, wrap construction, closure hardware, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 21-25 days after approvals.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Bagged with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers. Fold protects wrap structure; optional tissue.
