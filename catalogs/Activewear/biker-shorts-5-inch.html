Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs include inseam length, waist height, pocket placement, stitch specs, and GSM. Samples in 8-10 days with pocket/no-pocket variants and waistband tension options.

Making Process

Fabric Selection: 87% Nylon / 13% Spandex (230-250 GSM) with wicking; squat-proof density validated in lab dips.

Cut & Sew: Precision cutting for grain alignment; gusset insertion for mobility; pockets reinforced with bartacks.

Waistband: Non-roll elastic or bonded waist; tension graded per size; optional drawcord.

Finishing: Flatlock or coverstitch seams to minimize chafe; heat setting for length stability.

Quality Checks: AQL Level 2 with opacity/squat test, stretch recovery, seam strength, and shade review.

Sourcing

Certified nylon/spandex knit's, compliant elastics, and pocket linings; trims and labels from audited suppliers.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 600 pcs per color. Volume tiers at 2,000+ pcs reduce unit cost.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $2.28 - $2.97 including fabric, gusset, flatlock/coverstitch, optional pockets, QC, and packing. Pockets or bonded waist add modest premiums.

Production Time

Bulk: 19-25 days after approvals. Rush: 18-20 days on core colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging

Fold-pack polybags with barcode/ASIN, hang tags, size stickers, and care labels. Messaging can highlight squat-proof, pocketed, and wicking performance.
