Tech Pack Creation

Sheer pantyhose require precision tech pack documentation to achieve consistent sheerness and luxury feel. We detail denier targets (typically 15�40), opacity standards, size charts with precise fit tolerances, waistband specifications, reinforced toe options, wash/care requirements, and packaging details. Our documentation ensures that every pair delivers the whisper-soft, elegant feel that customers expect.

Sampling

Fit and sheerness samples arrive within 7�9 days. We evaluate denier consistency, waistband comfort, color accuracy, laddering resistance, and overall elegance. Samples are wash-tested to confirm durability and color hold. Adjustments to denier, waistband height, or reinforcement options are iterated quickly.

Making Process

Sheer pantyhose combine luxury with durability using specialized fine-denier knitting machines creating ultra-fine knits; premium nylon and spandex blends engineered for smoothness and strength; optional reinforced toe preventing runs at critical wear points; tuned waistbands preventing rolling while maintaining invisible, all-day comfort; heat-boarding locking in denier and preventing pilling; luxury dyes delivering rich, fade-resistant colors; and ladder-resistance testing confirming durability.

Sourcing

Sheer pantyhose demand premium materials. We source ultra-fine nylon and high-grade spandex from mills specializing in hosiery, comfort waistband elastics, reinforced toe yarns when applicable, luxury dyes for depth and richness, skin-tone labels, and elegant packaging. Our supply chain is transparent and ethically managed, supporting your brand's luxury positioning.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): Flexible Order Options

Standard MOQ is 400 pairs per color, reflecting fine-gauge knitting setup and dye vat requirements. Fashion and luxury brands often consolidate across multiple shades (black, nude, taupe, etc.) to optimize per-unit economics while building comprehensive color palettes.
