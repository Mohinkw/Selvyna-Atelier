Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, vest length, armhole depth, button placement, pocket placement, and back strap width. Fit'samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 95% polyester / 5% spandex suiting (220 GSM) for structure and comfort.

Construction: Tailored fit through shoulders; structured body; V-neck or round neck per design.

Details: Button front closure with quality buttons; chest and side pockets per spec; optional back strap for fit adjustment.

Lining: Full polyester or cotton lining for comfort and professional finish.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining structure; reinforced button holes; topstitched seams; QC for measurements and tailoring quality.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester suiting mills; quality buttons; lining fabric; labels within RSL.
