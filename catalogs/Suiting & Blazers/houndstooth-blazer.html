Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify shoulder width, chest width, blazer length, sleeve length, collar style, button placement, and houndstooth pattern scale. Fit samples in 12-14 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 60% polyester / 35% rayon / 5% spandex jacquard (240 GSM) with woven houndstooth pattern.

Pattern: Classic houndstooth weave per spec; quality yarn combination for dimensional appearance.

Construction: Tailored fit through shoulders and body; fitted sleeve length; structured collar per design.

Details: Single or double-breasted button closure per design; chest and side pockets; topstitched seams.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining pattern definition; reinforced button holes; quality lining; QC for pattern consistency and fit.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester-rayon jacquard mills with houndstooth pattern capability; quality buttons; lining; labels within RSL.
