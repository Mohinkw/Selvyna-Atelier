Premium Ribbed Racerback Tank Manufacturing in Bangladesh

Custom Private Label & Bulk Supply Solutions for Global Fashion Brands

Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs define rib gauge, armhole depth, neck width, shrink allowances, and label placement. Fit'samples in 8-10 days to validate coverage and recovery.

Making Process

Fabric: 180-200 GSM cotton/spandex rib with balanced stretch and rebound.

Cut & Sew: Grain-aligned cutting; binding or fold-over elastic at armhole/neck; bartack at racerback junction.

Stability: Stay-stitch necklines; optional clear elastic for added hold.

Finishing: Steam and measure; QC for recovery, neck symmetry, and shade.

Sourcing

Rib knit's from compliant mills; elastics nickel-safe; dyes meeting RSL; trims and labels traceable.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 750 pcs per color. Shared fabric base across neck depths possible.

Price Transparency

Approx. FOB: $1.88 - $2.62 including fabric, binding, bartacks, QC, and packing.

Production Time

Bulk: 20-20 days after approvals. Rush feasible on repeat colors.

Custom Labeling & Amazon FBA Ready Packaging
