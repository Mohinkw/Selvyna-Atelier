Tech Pack & Sampling

Tech packs specify chest width, body length, sleeve length, turtleneck height and circumference, gusset sizing, and snap/button placement. Fit samples in 11-13 days.

Making Process

Fabric: 92% polyester / 8% spandex jersey (210 GSM) for structure and sophistication.

Construction: Fitted fit through body; long sleeves; engineered turtleneck with proper height and circumference.

Turtleneck: Ribbed or seamless turtleneck per spec; quality rolling without bunching; comfortable fit around neck.

Gusset: Quality gusset construction for movement and comfort.

Closure: Quality snaps or buttons at crotch; reinforced placket.

Finishing: Careful press maintaining turtleneck shape; quality seaming; QC for neckline consistency and closure.

Sourcing

Compliant polyester-spandex jersey mills; quality ribbed fabric for turtleneck or seamless production; quality snaps; labels within RSL.
